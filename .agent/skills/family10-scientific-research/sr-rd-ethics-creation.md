---
name: R&D Ethics & Perception (Creation)
description: Briefing and methodology for perception of ethics, safety, and transparency in new innovation.
id: sr-rd-ethics-creation
---

<methodology>
1. **Innovation Safety Anchor**: Ensure the brief identifies the degree of safety and welcome felt by employees and the public toward new R&D.
2. **Ethics & Transparency Delta**: Instruction to evaluate the perception of the organizational culture and its impact on the energy of the innovation interaction.
3. **Internal Hero Identification**: determine if there are specific researcher behaviors or communication strategies that either encouraged or discouraged the trust in the R&D.
</methodology>

<coverage_tree>
- **Initial Impression**: First word or feeling that comes to mind when the R&D project is mentioned.
- **R&D Awareness**: Clarity and perceived relevance of the innovation objectives.
- **Institutional Trust**: Relationship with the researcher team and feeling of being valued as a stakeholder.
- **Public Policy Alignment**: perception of the innovation's ability to support the company's/community's core mission and goals.
- **Ethics Awareness**: The average level of recognition for the importance of transparency in the core mission.
- **Safety Perception**: Feeling of security during the innovation process.
- **Brand Reputation**: How the innovation represents the company brand and values in the community.
</coverage_tree>
