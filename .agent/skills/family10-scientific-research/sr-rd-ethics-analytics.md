# Section 1: What This Domain's Data Actually Means
R&D Ethics data is a leading indicator of catastrophic corporate failure. If the engineers building the software do not trust the software, the public eventually will not trust the software. The Analytics agent must synthesize the transcripts to measure the "Values vs Incentives Gap." It must ruthlessly expose "Ethics-Washing"—instances where a company uses PR to appear responsible while maintaining internal KPIs that reward reckless speed. The Analytics agent must flag 'Moral Injury' as the primary driver of top-tier talent churn in frontier tech.

# Section 2: Coverage Interpretation Guide
**The Safety Culture Matrix:**
- High Safety, Aligned Incentives: The Vanguard. The culture actually rewards engineers for hitting the brakes when something is wrong.
- High Safety, Misaligned Incentives: The Breaking Point. Engineers feel safe speaking up, but they watch their peers get promoted for ignoring safety. Safety culture will inevitably collapse.
- Low Safety, Aligned Incentives: The Mercenary Lab. Nobody speaks up, everybody ships fast, everybody gets paid. Extremely high regulatory/PR risk.
- Low Safety, Misaligned Incentives: The Toxic Void. Severe moral injury. Top talent treats it as a stepping stone and leaves as quickly as possible.

# Section 3: Quality Weighting Rules
**The 'Cassandra Ignored' Multiplier:** Feedback where an engineer explicitly states they accurately flagged a severe ethical/safety flaw, were actively ignored by management, and the flaw was shipped anyway, is weighted at 5x. This is qualitative proof that the company's safety apparatus is decorative, not functional.

# Section 4: Benchmark Context
**The "PR vs Product" Base:** The Analytics agent must determine if the company views ethics as a *Compliance/Marketing* function (managing public anger) or a *Product* function (building fundamentally safer things). If engineers view the Ethics Team as an extension of the PR department, the Analytics report must flag this as a structural failure.

# Section 5: Output Format Specification
**Section 1: Study Parameters**
Product boundary (e.g., LLM Training, Autonomous Driving), demographic bounds.
**Section 2: Executive Summary**
The definitive diagnosis of the Safety Culture Matrix (e.g., "A Severe Epidemic of Moral Injury and Ethics-Washing").
**Section 3: The 'Silence' Audit**
Specific measurement of *why* engineers are choosing not to report safety flaws (e.g., Fear of Retaliation vs Apathy/Assuming Nothing Will Change).
**Section 4: The Incentive Perversity Log**
Granular analysis of exactly how middle management's KPIs are overriding the CEO's stated safety values.
**Section 5: Decision Map Response**
Direct mapping to the reporting structures, incentive redesigns, or independent oversight boards requested in the brief.
**Section 6: Limitations and Confidence Notes**
Required section.

# Section 6: Multi-Session Analysis Guide
Analyze the "Resignation Threshold." If transcripts show that the primary reason senior engineers are quitting is *not* compensation, but specifically that they do not want to be legally or morally associated with the company's future product launches, the Analytics agent must flag this as a critical intellectual capital drain.

# Section 7: Flagging and Limitation Language
**When severe 'Metric Perversity' is detected:**
"Synthesis of the R&D Ethics data reveals a catastrophic 'Metric Perversity' rendering the company's stated ethical guardrails completely ineffective. Across [Percentage]% of evaluated engineers, there is a universal consensus that raising a safety concern is actively detrimental to their career advancement. While executive leadership promotes a 'Safety First' culture externally, middle management's bonus structure is exclusively tied to sprint velocity and launch dates. Consequently, the engineering cohort is actively burying safety concerns to meet artificial deadlines. We strongly advise an immediate severing of velocity-based KPIs for QA and Safety personnel, granting them structural independence and absolute veto power over product launches."
