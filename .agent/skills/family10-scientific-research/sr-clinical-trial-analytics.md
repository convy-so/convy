---
name: Clinical Trial Experience (Analytics)
description: Analysis of participant experience and ethics in clinical studies outcomes.
id: sr-clinical-trial-analytics
---

<methodology>
1. **The Participant-Safety Delta**: Analyze the alignment between the trial's intended "Safe Place" and the participant's reported security feeling post-discussion.
2. **Behavioral Attribution**: Distinguish between "Functional Failures" (informed consent, safety) and "Institutional Failures" (trust, lack of transparency).
3. **Internal Persistence Correlation**: Analyze how the consistency across touchpoints (Relational vs. Functional) correlates with the likelihood to continue with the study.
4. **Internal Hero Identification**: Pinpointing specific researcher behaviors or recognitions that drove the highest positive sentiment.
</methodology>

<insight_extraction>
- **The Trial Highlight**: What was the most successful part of the participant experience?
- **The Clinical Barrier**: Identification of any major points of failure or friction in the study path.
- **Institutional Status Mastery Score**: Overall qualitative grade of the researcher's readiness to deliver a premium identity.
- **Actionable Strategic Plan**: Identification of priority focus areas for future researcher training or study refinement.
</insight_extraction>
