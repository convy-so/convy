<methodology>
1. **The Hypothesis Delta**: Analyze the alignment between the original hypothesis and the actual reported data points post-usage period.
2. **Variable Attribution**: Metric assessment of how the Independent Variable influenced the Dependent Variable.
3. **Consistency Assessment**: Analyze the variance in response across participants to identify "outliers" or "significant patterns."
4. **Protocol Adherence Log**: Identification of any sessions where the student's behavior indicates a failure in the experimental setup.
</methodology>

<insight_extraction>
- **The Core Scientific Discovery**: What is the most significant finding?
- **Variable Impact Prototyping**: Compilation of how specific variables influenced the final outcome.
- **Mental Model Barriers**: Analysis of why the participant's expectation didn't match the experimental logic.
- **Actionable Methodological Plan**: Identification of priority focus areas for future research or study adjustment.
</insight_extraction>
