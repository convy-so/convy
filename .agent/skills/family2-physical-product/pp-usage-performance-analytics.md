---
name: Usage & Performance Research (Analytics)
description: Analysis of physical product usage and durability outcomes.
id: pp-usage-performance-analytics
---

<methodology>
1. **The Utility Delta**: Analyze the alignment between the product's intended usage and the actual reported utility post-usage period.
2. **Durability Attribution**: Distinguish between "Wear-and-tear" and "Build Quality Failures" based on the severity and frequency of issues.
3. **Workflow Integration Score**: Metric assessment of how naturally the product fit into the user's routine without requiring excessive accommodation.
4. **Relationship Transition**: Analyze whether the usage period moved the respondent toward advocacy (Promoter) or detachment (Detractor).
</methodology>

<insight_extraction>
- **The Utility Highlight**: What is the product's primary functional strength?
- **The Durability Risk**: Identification of any physical points of failure or weakness.
- **Workflow Friction**: Analysis of why the product became an "interruption" rather than an "aid" in the user's daily life.
- **Maintenance/Setup Barriers**: Analysis of the effort required for initial configuration and ongoing upkeep.
</insight_extraction>
