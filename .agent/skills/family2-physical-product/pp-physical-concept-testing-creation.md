---
name: Physical Concept Testing (Creation)
description: Briefing and methodology for new physical product concept validation.
id: pp-physical-concept-testing-creation
---

<methodology>
1. **Problem-Solution Fit**: Ensure the brief identifies the specific "Problem" the concept is intended to solve.
2. **Behavioral Anchor**: Instruction to evaluate the concept against the respondent's *current* behavior (not just theoretical liking).
3. **Credibility Elicitation**: Identify if the product's promise feels believable given the brand and physical design.
</methodology>

<coverage_tree>
- **Initial Resonance**: First reaction to the concept's core promise.
- **Problem Identification**: How well the respondent recognizes the problem being solved.
- **Usage Context**: Where and when the respondent imagines using the product.
- **Switching Barrier**: Perceived effort required to change from their current solution.
- **Price-Value Expectation**: Theoretical ROI and willingness to pay.
</coverage_tree>
