---
name: Sensory & Product Evaluation (Creation)
description: Briefing and methodology for physical product testing.
id: pp-sensory-evaluation-creation
---

<methodology>
1. **Sensory Anchoring**: Ensure the brief identifies the specific sensory focus: Taste, Scent, Texture, Sound, or Visual appeal.
2. **Blind vs. Branded**: Instruction to specify whether the respondent knows the brand (branded) or if it's a blind test.
3. **Usage Occasion**: Define the physical context of the test (e.g., Home use, Lab use, Transit).
</methodology>

<coverage_tree>
- **Initial Perception**: First reaction to packaging and unboxing.
- **Sensory Deep-Dive**: Specific ratings on the primary sensory attribute (e.g., sweetness, fragrance, durability).
- **Functional Performance**: How well the product performs its intended physical task.
- **Comparative Context**: Performance versus the respondent's current brand.
- **Future Intent**: Likelihood to purchase or replace existing products.
</coverage_tree>
