<methodology>
1. **Unboxing Journey**: Ensure the brief covers the sequential experience from first sight to disposal/storage.
2. **Shelf Impact**: Instruction to evaluate how the package stands out (or doesn't) against competitors in a simulated or real retail environment.
3. **Functional Messaging**: Identify if the packaging successfully communicates key product benefits without overwhelming the user.
</methodology>

<coverage_tree>
- **Visual Appeal**: First impression of colors, typography, and imagery.
- **Material Quality**: Perception of the physical materials (weight, texture, sustainability).
- **Ease of Opening**: Physical effort required to access the product.
- **Informational Clarity**: Success of the labeling in communicating usage and value.
- **Storage/Re-use**: Post-unboxing utility of the packaging.
</coverage_tree>
