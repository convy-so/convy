---

<methodology>
1. **The Shelf Impact Delta**: Analyze the alignment between the packaging's visual appeal and the respondent's reported shelf perception post-unboxing experience.
2. **Behavioral Attribution**: Distinguish between "Functional Packaging Failures" (couldn't open) and "Communicative Failures" (didn't understand the label).
3. **Material Sentiment Correlation**: Analyze how the perception of packaging materials correlates with the overall quality perception of the product.
4. **Disposal/Storage Pattern Assessment**: Identify the specific behaviors and sentiments that indicate packaging utility post-purchase.
</methodology>

<insight_extraction>
- **The Packaging Highlight**: What was the most successful part of the physical design?
- **The Functional Friction**: Identification of any physical barriers encountered during unboxing or usage.
- **Brand Messaging Mastery**: Overall qualitative grade of the packaging's readiness to support product value.
- **Actionable Design Plan**: Identification of priority focus areas for packaging improvement.
</insight_extraction>
