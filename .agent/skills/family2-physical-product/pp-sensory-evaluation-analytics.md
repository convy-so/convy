---
name: Sensory & Product Evaluation (Analytics)
description: Sensory profiling and brand-delta analysis for physical products.
id: pp-sensory-evaluation-analytics
---

<methodology>
1. **Sensory Intersection**: Correlate specific sensory descriptors (e.g., "crunchy," "aromatic") with overall purchase intent.
2. **The Brand Gap**: If the test is branded, analyze how the brand's stated promise matches the physical sensory experience.
3. **Consistency Assessment**: Analyze the variance in sensory perception across respondents to identify "polarizing" vs. "consistently high" attributes.
</methodology>

<insight_extraction>
- **The Signature Sensory**: What is the product's primary physical attractor?
- **Sensory Fatigue**: Identification of any attributes that were initially positive but became negative over time or during extended use.
- **The Competitive Edge**: Comparison of the product's sensory profile versus the leading benchmark.
- **Packaging/Ergonomic Fit**: Analysis of the physical interaction with the product's delivery mechanism.
</insight_extraction>
