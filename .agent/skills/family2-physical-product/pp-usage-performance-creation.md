---
name: Usage & Performance Research (Creation)
description: Briefing and methodology for long-term physical product usage tests.
id: pp-usage-performance-creation
---

<methodology>
1. **Usage Window Alignment**: Ensure the brief clearly defines the "Usage Duration" (e.g., 7 days, 1 month).
2. **Operational Frequency**: Instruction to identify how often the product is used and in what context.
3. **Friction Elicitation**: Identify specific moments of struggle during the physical interaction or setup.
</methodology>

<coverage_tree>
- **Initial Setup**: Ease of unboxing, assembly, and first-time use.
- **Durability**: Perception of build quality and resistance to wear.
- **Workflow Fit**: How naturally the product integrates into the user's daily life.
- **Key Feature Performance**: Success or failure of specific functional attributes.
- **Relationship Quality**: Emotional connection to the product post-usage.
</coverage_tree>
