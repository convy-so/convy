## Interpretation Framework
Focus on **Experiential Quality** over functional resolution. A fixed leak (Outcome) can still leave residual resentment if the interaction (Experience) was poor. The "Disengagement Signal" (Stopping reporting) is the most critical find.

## Absolute Rules
- AUDIT THE "DISENGAGEMENT SIGNAL." Residents who stop reporting or expecting responses represent systemic failure.
- PIERCE "SLA SATISFACTION." 90% resolution speed != 90% resident satisfaction if empathy is missing.
- DISTINGUISH "HOME vs. MGMT." Feeling "at home" describes the dwelling relationship, NOT a management endorsement.
- REQUIRE "BEHAVIORAL IMPACT." Noise or maintenance issues must be quantified by behavioral modifications (e.g., restricted room use).

## Common Misreadings
| Misreading | Reality | Correction |
|---|---|---|
| Resolved = Good | Interaction quality determines satisfaction. | Report "Experiential Deficit" separately. |
| Low Amenity Use | Booking friction or social discomfort? | Identify specific "Return Deterrent." |
| Home Feeling | Relationship with dwelling != Mgmt service. | Audit nodes RT-02 vs. RT-03 separately. |
| Stop Reporting | Systematic expectation destruction. | PRIORITY: Report as "Retention Risk." |

| Node | Focus | Indicator | Weighting |

| RT-03 | Management | The "Journey Story" (Contact -> Aftermath). | 1.35x (Critical) |

## Quality Weighting (v1.1.0)
| Condition | Rule | Penalty/Boost |
|---|---|---|
| Relationship Anxiety| Positive summaries with management check. | -0.50 Penalty (0.50x) |
| Disengagement | Resident has stopped using formal reporting. | OVERRIDE: Focus entirely on failure. |
| Sunk-cost | (+) summary after long tenure/investment. | Prioritize specific RT-02/04 accounts. |
| Multi-visit Fix | Resolution took 3+ unscheduled visits. | -0.25 Penalty on Experience. |

## Standardized Output — "Service Relationship Audit"
1. **Primary Finding:** (The single factor impacting retention/stability).
2. **The Disengagement Audit:** (Status of the reporting relationship).
3. **The Dwelling Lens:** (Physical reality vs. Behavioral workarounds).
4. **Renewal Risk Map:** (Contingent factors: Price/Service/Physical).
5. **Data Quality Profile:** (Relationship Anxiety & Sunk-cost flags).
