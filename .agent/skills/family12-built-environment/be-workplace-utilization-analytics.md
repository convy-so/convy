## Interpretation Framework
Behavioral evidence (moves/workarounds) always supersedes evaluative statements ("The office is fine"). If an employee says the office is great but consistently works from home for deep focus, the office is failing its "Product-Market Fit" for that work mode.

## Absolute Rules
- WORKAROUNDS = FINDINGS. Any space used for non-intended purpose (e.g., booth for solo work) is a primary design gap.
- PATTERN > INCIDENT. Multiple AV failures = "Technology Design Failure," not a maintenance ticket.
- HYBRID EQUITY. Audit the "Two-Tier" experience where remote callers are socially/technologically sidelined.
- ATTENDANCE DRIVERS. Connect every friction point to its impact on the "Home vs. Office" decision (Node 07).

## Common Misreadings
| Misreading | Reality | Correction |
|---|---|---|
| Silence = Success | Stress/Mandates suppress criticism. | Weight workarounds at 1.0x, praise at 0.5x. |
| Focus = Preference | Systemic under-provision of quiet. | Report as "Ratio Mismatch" design finding. |
| Tech = IT Issue | AV failures deter attendance. | Report as "Attendance Friction" finding. |
| Low Use = Attitude | Office lacks a "Commute Justifier." | Report as "Product-Market Fit" failure. |

## Node Interpretation & Weights
| Node | Focus | Indicator |
|---|---|---|
| WO-01 | Spatial Map | Moves/Avoidance patterns in the "Journey." |
| WO-02 | Focus Gap | Solo work relocation to booths/meeting rooms. |
| WO-03 | Collab | Planned vs. Spontaneous quality variance. |
| WO-04 | Hybrid | "Two-Tier" status; defaulting to laptops over AV. |
| WO-07 | Trade-off | Rank "Home-Wins" vs. "Office-Wins" factors. |

## Quality Weighting (v1.1.0)
| Condition | Rule | Weighting |
|---|---|---|
| RTO Tension | Flag if mandate/stress exists. | Global Praise: 0.5x / Workarounds: 1.0x |
| Narrative Gap | Praise vs. Specific Workaround. | Behavioral Reality overrides evaluative summary. |
| Compound Failure | Multiple tech failures in one session. | Cumulative "Confidence Loss" (Report as Pattern). |

## Standardized Output — "Executive Equity Summary"
1. **The Primary Gap:** (e.g., "The Focus Work Mismatch").
2. **The Workaround Pattern:** (Frequency/Type of substitution).
3. **The Hybrid Equity Score:** (Parity vs. Two-Tier status).
4. **Attendance Impact:** (Which specific node is driving the Home-Win).
5. **Physical Intervention:** (Design requirement, NOT policy change).

## Flagging Templates
**THE FOCUS GAP:**
> "The focus work gap identified—[workarounds/cause]—is an attendance driver. Until addressed, the office value proposition remains limited to collaboration-only."

**THE TWO-TIER MEETING:**
> "Technology created a two-tier meeting experience where remote callers had inferior [audio/visibility] compared to in-room participants. This represents a systematic hybrid equity failure."

**RTO TENSION NOTE:**
> "This session carries RTO tension. Global assessments have been weighted at 0.5; specific behavioral workarounds have been taken as primary evidence."
