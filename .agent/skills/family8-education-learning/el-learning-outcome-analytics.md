## Interpretation Framework
**Goal:** Isolate the actual "Behavioral Delta" in the workplace.
**Type A (Behavioral Proof):** Walk-through of specific application (LO-02). High-confidence ROI.
**Type B (Dispositional Shift):** "Feeling more aware" (LO-03). Medium-confidence context.
**Type C (Knowledge Retention):** Can explain theory (LO-01) but zero use. "Potential Transfer."
**Type D (Org Resignation):** Knows skill but blocked by system (LO-06). "Systemic ROI Block."

## Critical Misreadings
- **Academic Success:** High LO-01 + Zero LO-02 = Inactive Academic (ROI is zero).
- **Frequency Claims:** "I use it all the time" without a walk-through = Positivity Performance (Low confidence).
- **Manager Block:** High capability + No time/support = Curriculum success / Org failure.

## Evidence Hierarchy
| Tier | Title | Evidence Type |
|---|---|---|
| Tier 1 | The Walk-through (1.00) | Logically chronological behavioral success story. |
| Tier 2 | Structural Barrier (0.90) | Verifiable blocker (Software/Process incompatibility). |
| Tier 3 | Comparative Change (0.75) | "Before I did X, now I do Y" delta description. |
| Tier 4 | Theory Recall (0.50) | Memory of concepts without application instances. |
| Tier 5 | SD Performance (0.25) | Atmospheric/General positive claims ("Very useful"). |

## Synthesis & Reporting Patterns
| Pattern | Signal | Action |
|---|---|---|
| Theoretical Success | High LO-01 / Zero LO-02 | Skill is known but inactive. |
| Barrier Blocked | High LO-08 / High LO-06 | Capability exist but ROI inhibited by friction. |
| Habitual Champion | High LO-02 + High LO-05 | Successful transfer + habit formation. |
| Intuitive Mastery | Low LO-01 / High LO-02 | Theory is lost but skill is being used (Muscle memory). |

## Scoring Weighting
- **Barrier Bonus (+0.20):** Prioritize findings mentioning specific Org Barriers (LO-06).
- **Recency Penalty (-0.15):** If Window > 90 days, discount LO-01; focus strictly on LO-05 (Habit).

## Reporting Artifacts
**Part 1: Executive Summary** (The Behavioral ROI).
**Part 2: Structural Findings** (Successful Transfers & Active Barriers).
**Part 3: Skill Decay Profile** (What was lost over the 30-90 day window).
**Part 4: Recommendations** (Process vs. Training interventions).

## Escalation Protocols
- **Zero-Transfer Signal:** First 5 sessions show no Tier 1 or Tier 3 behavioral evidence.
- **Toxic Barrier:** Manager/Dept actively penalizing use of new training.

## Reporting Flag Templates
**POSITIVITY BIAS:**
> "The respondent reports 'high utility' (LO-08) but was unable to provide a single specific instance of application (LO-02). This represents 'Positivity Performance'—the desire to appear professional despite zero behavioral transfer."

**STRUCTURAL BLOCK:**
> "Transfer evidence is high (Tier 2/3) but frequency (LO-05) is low due to [Specific Barrier]. The training has successfully built capability, but the organization is preventing the ROI from being realized."
