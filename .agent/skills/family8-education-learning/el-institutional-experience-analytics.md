## Interpretation Framework
**Goal:** Identify "Structural Resignation" (normalizing systemic failure).
**Lens A (Functional):** Process/Admin failure ("Portal was down").
**Lens B (Identity):** Structural alienation ("Only one with my background").
**Critical Alignment:** When Functional friction is interpreted as Identity indifference (e.g. "Office didn't call back because I'm part-time").

## Critical Misreadings
- **"Independent" Signal:** Masking isolation (IE-03). Cross-reference with IE-05 (Valued).
- **"Great Degree":** Credential Defense. Disregard global satisfaction; focus on journey nodes (IE-01/02).
- **"First-Year Fog":** Administrative excuse. "Fog" > 14 days = failure (IE-01).

## Institutional Lifecycle Calibrations
- **New Starter:** Focal point: Welcome/Trust/IE-01.
- **Final Year:** Focal point: Brand/Value/IE-06.
*Note: Never aggregate different lifecycle stages into a single score.*

## Evidence Hierarchy
| Tier | Title | Evidence Type |
|---|---|---|
| Tier 1 | Narrative Proof (1.00) | Specific process narrative + office/outcome. |
| Tier 2 | Identity Anchor (0.90) | Personalization evidence (staff remembered detail). |
| Tier 3 | Expectation Gap (0.80) | Marketing expectation vs. Lived reality. |
| Tier 4 | General Sentiment (0.50)| "The atmosphere is welcoming" (Un-grounded). |
| Tier 5 | Compliance (0.30) | "No complaints, everything is fine." |

## Synthesis & Reporting Patterns
| Pattern | Signal | Action |
|---|---|---|
| Efficient Factory | High IE-02 / Low IE-05 | Focus on lack of personalization. |
| Social Sanctuary | High IE-03 / Low IE-02 | Focus on administrative chaos. |
| Broken Promise | High IE-06 / Low IE-01-05 | Focus on Brand vs. Reality gap. |

## Scoring Adjustments
- **First-Gen Bonus (+0.10):** Apply if first-gen student blames the *system* (overcomes self-blame bias).
- **Prestige Discount (0.85):** Apply to global positive assessments of elite/high-prestige institutions.

## Reporting Artifacts
**Part 1: Executive Summary** (The Brand-Experience Gap).
**Part 2: Structural Findings** (Administrative Loops & Social Islets).
**Part 3: Attrition Risks** (Specific withdrawal triggers).
**Part 4: Recommendations** (Process vs. Culture interventions).

## Escalation Protocols
- **High Withdrawal Trigger:** Multiple cohort reports of zero social connection (IE-03) + blocked admin (IE-02).
- **Structural Exclusion:** Findings suggest systematic bias in support service access.

## Reporting Flag Templates
**AUTHORITY DEFERENCE:**
> "The respondent exhibited 'Authority Deference' patterns (short, generic positive answers). While no explicit failures were reported, the absence of specific narratives suggests the respondent may not feel safe disclosing friction. Treat positive scores as conservative."

**PROCESS DESPAIR:**
> "The respondent describes a 'Structural Resignation' to administrative failure ('that's just how it is'). This indicates that the friction has been normalized by the student body and represents a significant long-term brand risk."
