## Interpretation Framework
**Goal:** Pierce "Completion Bias" to find "Residual Uncertainty."
**Type A (Cognitive/Structural):** Evidence of skill/logic application (Full weight).
**Type B (Affective/Instructional):** Assessment of delivery/instructor (0.75 weight).
**Type C (Atmospheric):** Generic positive compliance (0.50 weight).
**Signal:** High Type B/C with low Type A = "Engagement Masking" (Efficacy Failure).

## Critical Misreadings
- **10/10 Confidence:** If no behavioral grounding (CE-02), flag as "Overconfidence Gap."
- **"Perfect Clarity":** Lack of difficulty points signals low candor or shallow engagement.
- **Completion Bias:** "I finished everything" is not a learning outcome.

## Evidence Hierarchy
| Tier | Title | Evidence Type |
|---|---|---|
| Tier 1 | Behavioral Application (1.00) | Logic-based walk-through of a specific task. |
| Tier 2 | Content Friction (0.90) | Identifies "Mastery Wall" (Location + Cause). |
| Tier 3 | Comparative Assessment (0.80) | Pre- vs. Post-course state-change narrative. |
| Tier 4 | Patterned General (0.60) | "Exercises were the best part." |
| Tier 5 | Atmospheric (0.45) | "It was very professional." |

## Synthesis & Reporting Patterns
| Pattern | Signal | Action |
|---|---|---|
| Theoretical Success | High CE-01 / Low CE-02 | Skill is known but not ready for use. |
| Delivery success | High CE-04 / Low CE-03 | Instruction was liked; content was confusing. |
| Audience Mismatch | High friction in basic areas | Prerequisite or level adjustment needed. |

## Scoring Weighting
- **Mandatory Discount (0.75):** Apply to positive assessments if motivation is Mandatory.
- **Self-Paid Bonus (+0.15):** Apply to criticisms (higher Dissatisfaction threshold).

## Reporting Artifacts
**Part 1: Executive Summary** (Equipped vs. Happy balance).
**Part 2: Structural Findings** (Mastery Walls & Success Anchors).
**Part 3: Recommendations** (Curriculum vs. Delivery).
**Part 4: Limitations** (Recency factors & self-report limits).

## Escalation Protocols
- **Systemic Mastery Failure:** First 3 sessions show zero Tier 1 evidence on critical objectives.
- **Trust Breach:** Massive gap between marketing/description and actual content.

## Reporting Flag Templates
**MASTERY INFLATION:**
> "The respondent reported a confidence score of [X], but was unable to provide a behavioral walk-through of the application (CE-02). This represents 'Mastery Inflation' typical of post-completion sentiment and should not be used as evidence of organizational capability."

**STRUCTURAL FRICTION:**
> "Findings on [Module Name] indicate a structural 'Mastery Wall.' The friction described is not a factor of learner effort but a limitation of the instructional design in bridging [Concept A] to [Concept B]."
