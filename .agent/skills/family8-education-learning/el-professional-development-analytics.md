## Interpretation Framework
**Goal:** Distinguish "L&D Consumption" (classes) from "Structural Evolution" (advancement).
**Lens A (Advocacy):** Manager-opened opportunities (PD-05). High-confidence growth data.
**Lens B (Admin):** Status-update 1-on-1s (PD-04). Low-confidence development data (Performance audit).

## Critical Misreadings
- **Polite Stagnation:** Support without Advocacy (PD-05) = Zero growth.
- **Resource Mirage:** High library quality (PD-02) + No time to use it (PD-03) = Zero permission.
- **Coping Mask:** "I drive my own growth" + Low retention (PD-08) = Abandonment by organization.

## Evidence Hierarchy
| Tier | Title | Evidence Type |
|---|---|---|
| Tier 1 | Advocacy Instance (1.00) | Specific action: "Manager introduced me to X for Y visibility." |
| Tier 2 | Cultural Blocker (0.90) | Verifiable friction: "If seen not coding, I'm questioned." |
| Tier 3 | Path Clarity (0.75) | Documentary clarity on next level skills/KPIs. |
| Tier 4 | General Sentiment (0.50) | Atmospheric/General claims ("I feel like I'm growing"). |
| Tier 5 | Evaluative Performance (0.25) | Defensive/Safe performance ("Everything is fine"). |

## Synthesis & Reporting Patterns
| Pattern | Signal | Action |
|---|---|---|
| Invisible Ceiling | High PD-01 (Clarity) / Low PD-05 (Advocacy) | Path is clear but doors are locked. |
| Resource Mirage | High PD-02 (Resources) / Low PD-03 (Permission) | Theoretical support / Real-world friction. |
| Flight Risk Engine | Low PD-07 (Culture) / Low PD-08 (Retention) | Growth gap triggering exit intention. |
| Strategic Alignment | High confidence + Specific next step named | Successful growth engine at work. |

## Scoring Weighting
- **Manager Bonus (+0.25):** Prioritize PD-04/05 as #1 predictor of retention.
- **Tenure Penalty (-0.10):** Discount clarity (PD-01) for <6 month "Onboarding Optimism."

## Reporting Artifacts
**Part 1: Executive Summary** (The Growth Engine Audit).
**Part 2: Structural Findings** (Path Clarity & Manager Gaps).
**Part 3: Retention Vulnerabilities** (Where the gap triggers exits).
**Part 4: Recommendations** (Policy vs. Leadership interventions).

## Escalation Protocols
- **Systemic Advocacy Failure:** High-performers reporting actively blocked opportunities.
- **Disenchantment Signal:** Specific demographic (e.g. Mid-level women) reporting zero path visibility.

## Reporting Flag Templates
**AUTHORITY DEFERENCE:**
> "The respondent exhibited high 'Authority Deference' (brief, positive generalities). These scores should be viewed as 'Safe Performance' rather than operational truth. Findings on Manager Advocacy (PD-05) are inconclusive."

**RESIGNATION:**
> "The data suggests 'Growth Resignation.' The respondent uses pragmatic, low-energy language ('I just do it myself') indicating they no longer expect the organization to contribute to their evolution."
