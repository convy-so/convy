---
name: B2B Service Delivery (Creation)
description: Briefing and methodology for customer experience during professional service projects.
id: b2b-service-delivery-creation
---

<methodology>
1. **On-Time & Quality Delta**: Ensure the brief identifies the "Delta" between the company's intended delivery and the client's reported quality and speed.
2. **Project Experience Anchor**: Instruction to evaluate the perception of the professional service project execution and its impact on the energy of the client relationship.
3. **Implicit Success Mapping**: determine if there are specific patterns or behaviors that reflect an underlying belief in the company's ability to deliver a premium service.
</methodology>

<coverage_tree>
- **Initial Impression**: First word or feeling that comes to mind when the project is mentioned.
- **On-Time Delivery**: perception of the company's ability to meet deadlines and project milestones.
- **Service Quality**: How well the company's delivery is perceived to match the business needs.
- **Relational Trust**: Relationship with the project team and feeling of being valued as a client.
- **Strategic Value**: Perception of the service's ability to support the company's core mission and goals.
- **Brand Reputation**: How the project delivery represents the company brand and values.
- **Post-Delivery Sentiment**: Overall qualitative grade of the company's readiness to deliver a premium service identity.
- **Recommendation Likelihood**: Likelihood of the client to recommend the company's professional services to others.
</coverage_tree>
