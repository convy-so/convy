name: B2B Service Delivery (Analytics)

---

<methodology>
1. **The Project-Delivery Delta**: Analyze the alignment between the company's intended "Safe Place" and the client's reported emotion post-delivery.
2. **Behavioral Attribution**: Distinguish between "Functional Failures" (on-time delivery, service quality) and "Relational Failures" (trust, collaboration with the project team).
3. **Service Persistence Correlation**: Analyze how the consistency across touchpoints (Relational vs. Functional) correlates with the likelihood to re-engage with the professional services.
4. **Internal Hero Identification**: Pinpointing specific project team behaviors or recognitions that drove the highest positive sentiment or reliability.
</methodology>

<insight_extraction>
- **The Service Highlight**: What was the most successful part of the delivery experience?
- **The Professional Barrier**: Identification of any major points of failure or friction in the project path.
- **Brand Status Mastery Score**: Overall qualitative grade of the company's readiness to deliver a premium identity.
- **Actionable Strategic Plan**: Identification of priority focus areas for future project training or service refinement.
</insight_extraction>
