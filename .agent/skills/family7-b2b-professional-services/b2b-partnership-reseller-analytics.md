name: Reseller & Partner Research (Analytics)
description: Analysis of relationship with distributors and indirect channel partners outcomes.

---

<methodology>
1. **The Channel-Support Delta**: Analyze the alignment between the company's intended "Enablement" and the partner's reported support feeling.
2. **Behavioral Attribution**: Distinguish between "Functional Failures" (tools, training, operational efficiency) and "Relational Failures" (trust, strategic alliance).
3. **Partner Persistence Correlation**: Analyze how the consistency across touchpoints (Relational vs. Functional) correlates with the likelihood to stick with the company.
4. **Internal Hero Identification**: Pinpointing specific partner support behaviors or recognitions that drove the highest positive sentiment.
</methodology>

<insight_extraction>
- **The Partner Highlight**: What was the most successful part of the channel experience?
- **The Enablement Barrier**: Identification of any major points of failure or friction in the partner path.
- **Brand Reputation Mastery Score**: Overall qualitative grade of the company's readiness to deliver a premium identity.
- **Actionable Strategic Plan**: Identification of priority focus areas for future partner training or process refinement.
</insight_extraction>
