name: B2B Partnership Health (Analytics)
description: Analysis of long-term business-to-business relationship quality outcomes.
id: b2b-partnership-health-analytics
---

<methodology>
1. **The Partnership Delta**: Analyze the alignment between the partner's intended "Safe Place" and the company's reported emotion post-discussion.
2. **Behavioral Attribution**: Distinguish between "Functional Failures" (operational efficiency, reliability) and "Relational Failures" (strategic alignment, trust).
3. **Partnership Persistence Correlation**: Analyze how the consistency across touchpoints (Relational vs. Functional) correlates with the likelihood to stick with the partner.
4. **Internal Hero Identification**: Pinpointing specific partner behaviors or recognitions that drove the highest positive sentiment.
</methodology>

<insight_extraction>
- **The Partnership Highlight**: What was the most successful part of the B2B experience?
- **The Relational Barrier**: Identification of any major points of failure or friction in the partnership path.
- **Brand Reputation Mastery Score**: Overall qualitative grade of the partner's readiness to deliver a premium identity.
- **Actionable Strategic Plan**: Identification of priority focus areas for future partnership building or process refinement.
</insight_extraction>
