name: B2B Buying Process (Creation)
description: Briefing and methodology for complex procurement journeys and decision-making units.
id: b2b-buying-process-creation
---

<methodology>
1. **Decision-Making Unit Mapping**: Ensure the brief identifies the different roles (Influencer, Decision-Maker, User, Gatekeeper) and their unique impact on the journey.
2. **Procurement Friction Anchor**: Instruction to evaluate the perception of the buyer's process and its impact on the energy of the vendor selection.
3. **Implicit Value Mapping**: Determine if there are specific patterns or associations that reflect an underlying belief in the vendor's commitment to the buyer's success.
</methodology>

<coverage_tree>
- **Initial Awareness**: First moment or event that triggered the thought of a new solution.
- **RFP & Selection Process**: Ease of navigating the procurement journey and the quality of vendor proposals.
- **Influencer Impact**: How well the different members of the decision-making unit were perceived to align.
- **Vendor Reliability**: Perception of the vendor's ability to deliver on-time and with high-quality results.
- **Contract & Compliance**: Ease of navigating the legal and financial hurdles of the purchase.
- **Sense of Pride**: How the buyer feels about their new solution and its reputation.
- **Future Commitment**: Likelihood of the company continuing the vendor relationship in the long-term.
</coverage_tree>
