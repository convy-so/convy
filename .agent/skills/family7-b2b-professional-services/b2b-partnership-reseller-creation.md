---
name: Reseller & Partner Research (Creation)
description: Briefing and methodology for relationship with distributors and indirect channel partners.
id: b2b-partnership-reseller-creation
---

<methodology>
1. **Channel-Value Delta**: Ensure the brief identifies the "Delta" between the company's intended channel support and the partner's reported enablement feeling.
2. **Operational Reliability Anchor**: Instruction to evaluate the perception of the partner's ability to represent the brand and deliver on-time results.
3. **Implicit Trust Mapping**: determine if there are specific patterns or behaviors that reflect an underlying belief in the partner's commitment to the success of the relationship.
</methodology>

<coverage_tree>
- **Initial Impression**: First word or feeling that comes to mind when the partner/distributor is mentioned.
- **Support & Enablement**: How well the company is perceived to provide the necessary tools and training for the partner's success.
- **Relational Trust**: Relationship with the partner and feeling of being valued as a strategic ally.
- **Operational Efficiency**: Speed, professionalisms, and reliability of the channel delivery.
- **Innovation & Proactivity**: sense of the partner's readiness to suggest new ideas or solve potential problems.
- **Brand Reputation**: How the partner represents the company brand and values in the market.
- **Retention Efficacy**: Likelihood of the partner continuing the relationship in the long-term.
</coverage_tree>
