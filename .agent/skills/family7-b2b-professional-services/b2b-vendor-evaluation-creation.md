---
name: Vendor & Supplier Evaluation (Creation)
description: Briefing and methodology for performance and reliability of B2B service providers.
id: b2b-vendor-evaluation-creation
---

<methodology>
1. **Performance & Reliability Delta**: Ensure the brief identifies the "Delta" between the company's intended performance and the vendor's reported reliability.
2. **Technical & Relationship Anchor**: Instruction to evaluate the perception of the vendor's technical delivery and its impact on the energy of the partnership.
3. **Implicit Trust Mapping**: determine if there are specific patterns or behaviors that reflect an underlying belief in the vendor's commitment to the relationship.
</methodology>

<coverage_tree>
- **Initial Impression**: First word or feeling that comes to mind when the vendor is mentioned.
- **Support & Reliability**: How well the vendor is perceived to deliver on-time and with high-quality results.
- **Technical Performance**: perception of the vendor's ability to solve potential problems and suggest new ideas.
- **Relational Trust**: Relationship with the vendor and feeling of being valued as a client.
- **Strategic Alignment**: Perception of the vendor's ability to support the company's core mission and goals.
- **Brand Reputation**: How the vendor represents the brand and values (if applicable).
- **Retention Efficacy**: Likelihood of the company continuing the vendor relationship in the long-term.
</coverage_tree>
