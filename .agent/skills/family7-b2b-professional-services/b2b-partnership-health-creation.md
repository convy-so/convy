---
name: B2B Partnership Health (Creation)
description: Briefing and methodology for long-term business-to-business relationship quality.
id: b2b-partnership-health-creation
---

<methodology>
1. **Strategic Alignment Delta**: Ensure the brief identifies the "Delta" between the partner's intended goals and the joint achievement.
2. **Operational Reliability Anchor**: Instruction to evaluate the perception of the partner's ability to deliver on-time and with high-quality results.
3. **Implicit Trust Mapping**: Determine if there are specific patterns or behaviors that reflect an underlying belief in the partner's commitment to the relationship.
</methodology>

<coverage_tree>
- **Initial Impression**: First word or feeling that comes to mind when the partner is mentioned.
- **Relational Trust**: How well the partner is perceived to have the company's best interests at heart.
- **Strategic Alignment**: Perception of the partner's ability to support the company's core mission and goals.
- **Operational Efficiency**: Speed, professionalisms, and reliability of the partnership delivery.
- **Innovation & Proactivity**: sense of the partner's readiness to suggest new ideas or solve potential problems.
- **Brand Reputation**: How the partner represents the company brand and values (if applicable).
- **Retention Efficacy**: Likelihood of the company continuing the partnership in the long-term.
</coverage_tree>
