name: Vendor & Supplier Evaluation (Analytics)
description: Analysis of performance and reliability of B2B service providers outcomes.
id: b2b-vendor-evaluation-analytics
---

<methodology>
1. **The Performance Delta**: Analyze the alignment between the vendor's intended "Safe Place" and the company's reported emotion post-discussion.
2. **Behavioral Attribution**: Distinguish between "Functional Failures" (technical performance, reliability) and "Relational Failures" (trust, strategic alignment).
3. **Vendor Persistence Correlation**: Analyze how the consistency across touchpoints (Relational vs. Functional) correlates with the likelihood to stick with the vendor.
4. **Internal Hero Identification**: Pinpointing specific vendor-related behaviors or recognitions that drove the highest positive sentiment.
</methodology>

<insight_extraction>
- **The Performance Highlight**: What was the most successful part of the vendor experience?
- **The Procurement Barrier**: Identification of any major points of failure or friction in the vendor path.
- **Brand Reputation Mastery Score**: Overall qualitative grade of the vendor's readiness to deliver a premium identity.
- **Actionable Strategic Plan**: Identification of priority focus areas for future vendor training or process refinement.
</insight_extraction>
