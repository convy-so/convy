## Interpretation Framework
**Goal:** Audit the "Operational Reality Gap." Distinguish between "Aspirational Values" (Marketing) and "Behavioral Norms" (Workday Reality).
**Key Metric:** **The Gap Score.** Numeric delta between values claimed and behaviors rewarded.
**Principle:** **The Survivor Premium.** Upweight (1.4x) descriptions of internal "workarounds" as reliable structural failure indicators.
**Quality Rule:** **The Hypocrisy Multiplier.** Downweight (0.65x) respondents who recite values without behavioral proof.

## Critical Misreadings
- **The Perk Illusion:** Mistaking beanbags/cereal/free lunch for a healthy behavioral culture.
- **Compliant Error:** Misreading silence as health; in fear cultures, "No News" is a severe warning.
- **High-Stakes Bias:** Mistaking revenue success for cultural health (often achieved via Fear-Driven Extraction).
- **Isolation Error:** Treating executive optimism (100% positive) as valid when ground-floor is 80% negative (Severe Isolation).

## The Apathy Metric (Surrender vs. Struggle)
| Archetype | Signal | Interpretation |
|---|---|---|
| Constructive Friction | High Frustration / High Fixed-Desire | **Healthy Growth.** Staff still cares enough to fight. |
| Defensive Survival | High Fear / High Workarounds | **Shadow Culture.** Survival strategies have bypassed formal rules. |
| Total Apathy | No Anger / No Hope / Compliance | **Institutional Death.** surrendering to friction; zero innovation. |
| Hostage Actor | 90%+ corporate jargon / PE Mask | **Performative Compliance.** Low diagnostic confidence. |

## Synthesis & Reporting Patterns
| Pattern | Signal | Action |
|---|---|---|
| Reward Gap | Promoted traits != Stated values | Culture Bankruptcy: Force alignment of rewards with values. |
| Witch Hunt | High blame during project failure | Safety Failure: Psych safety floor collapsed; innovation paralyzed. |
| Bureaucracy Base | Spend/Decision cycle > Industry | Process Paralysis: Name the top 3 structural blockers (Section 6). |
| Shadow Rule | Same survival hack cited by 30% | Confirmed Norm: Formalize the hack or kill the rule. |

## Synthesis Protocols
- **Step 1 (The Reality Delta):** Numeric score of the gap between poster and floor.
- **Step 2 (The Rule Audit):** Identify top 3 "Shadow Rules" (Unwritten rules for survival).
- **Step 3 (The Blame Audit):** Link risk-aversion to specific "Witch Hunt" history.
- **Step 4 (The Blind Spot):** Quantify the "Executive Blindness Index" (How much leadership is being lied to).

## Reporting Artifacts
**Part 1: Study Parameters** (Trigger, Outlier teams, Taboo map status).
**Part 2: Executive Gap Score** (The delta between claimed values and rewarded behaviors).
**Part 3: The Shadow Ruleset** (Top 3 survival strategies required to succeed in this company).
**Part 4: Structural Blockers** (Specific processes or norms causing cultural rot).
**Part 5: Leadership Blind Spot Index** (Measuring the feedback isolation of the executive team).

## Flagging Templates

**EXECUTIVE BLINDNESS:**
> "Total misalignment between leadership perception and ground-floor 'Survival Tactics'. Leadership is operating without a realistic feedback loop."

**INSTITUTIONAL APATHY:**
> "Apathy threshold reached 60% in [Segment]. Middle management has surrendered to friction. Imminent operational collapse risk."
