---
name: 360-Degree Feedback (Creation)
description: Briefing and methodology for multi-rater feedback for individual growth and performance.
id: wo-360-feedback-creation
---

<methodology>
1. **Multi-Rater Hierarchy**: Ensure the brief identifies the different perspectives (Peer, Manager, Self, Direct Report) and their unique value to the feedback.
2. **Growth-Mindset Focused**: Instruction to prioritize constructive, action-oriented feedback rather than just a purely evaluative score.
3. **Implicit Bias Identification**: Determine if there are specific patterns or associations that may reflect an underlying bias in the feedback.
</methodology>

<coverage_tree>
- **Core Competencies**: Feedback on the respondent's technical skills and performance.
- **Interpersonal Effectiveness**: perception of the respondent's communication and collaboration style.
- **Leadership & Influence**: Impact on the team's direction and energy (if applicable).
- **Self-Awareness Delta**: Alignment between the respondent's self-assessment and the ratings of others.
- **Actionable Growth Potential**: Identification of specific areas for future professional development.
- **Efficacy Belief**: The respondent's confidence in their ability to improve based on the feedback.
</coverage_tree>
