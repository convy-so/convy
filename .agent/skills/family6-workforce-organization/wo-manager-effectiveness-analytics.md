---
name: Manager Effectiveness (Analytics)
description: Analysis of direct supervisor impact and leadership quality outcomes.
id: wo-manager-effectiveness-analytics
---

<methodology>
1. **The Leadership Delta**: Analyze the alignment between the manager's intended support and the employee's reported support feeling.
2. **Behavioral Attribution**: Distinguish between "Functional Failures" (direction, coaching) and "Relational Failures" (trust, empathy).
3. **Internal Commitment Correlation**: Analyze how the consistency across touchpoints (Relational vs. Functional) correlates with the likelihood to stick with the organization.
4. **Leadership Hero Identification**: Pinpointing specific manager behaviors or recognitions that drove the highest positive sentiment.
</methodology>

<insight_extraction>
- **The Leadership Highlight**: What was the most successful part of the leadership experience?
- **The Management Barrier**: Identification of any major points of failure or friction in the manager path.
- **Support-Mastery Score**: Overall qualitative grade of the company's readiness to deliver a premium internal identity.
- **Actionable Strategic Plan**: Identification of priority focus areas for future manager training or leadership refinement.
</insight_extraction}
