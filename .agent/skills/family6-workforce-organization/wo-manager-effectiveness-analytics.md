## Interpretation Framework
**Goal:** Audit the "Operational Multiplier Effect." Prioritize functional output (Force Map) over interpersonal likeability.
**Key Metric:** **The Leadership Delta.** Gap between manager's "Claimed Style" and direct-report's "Lived Reality."
**Principle:** **The Shield Premium.** Upweight (1.3x) instances of active shielding from organizational noise.
**Quality Rule:** **The Bad News Penalty.** Downweight (0.80x) effectiveness if manager reacts to failure by blaming individuals (WO-05).

## Critical Misreadings
- **The Perfect Boss Trap:** Mistaking 100% positive/generic feedback for health; usually indicates "High Paranoia."
- **High-Stakes Error:** Mistaking revenue success for healthy management; often achieved via "Burnout-Driven Extraction."
- **Absence != Autonomy:** Treating a lack of presence as "Empowerment." Absence during crisis is "Leadership Neglect."
- **SME Bias:** Conflating technical genius with leadership ability. A genius developer can be a "Process Bottleneck."

## The Conflict Profile (Reaction to Bad News)
| Archetype | Signal | Interpretation |
|---|---|---|
| The Absorber | Calms team / Analyzes data | **High Potential.** Force multiplier in crisis. |
| The Passthrough | Forwards Exec panic | **Structural Failure.** No filtering/protection. |
| The Finger-Pointer | Immediate blame hunt | **Toxicity.** Destroys safety and innovation. |
| The Ghost | Absent during failure | **Leadership Neglect.** High accountability risk. |

## Synthesis & Reporting Patterns
| Pattern | Signal | Action |
|---|---|---|
| Intent-Impact Gap| "Coaching" vs. "Micromanagement"| Blind Spot Map: Force behavioral adjustment (Section 6). |
| Managing Up Tax | 20%+ energy on "Optical Work" | Inefficiency Audit: Quantify the hourly cost of the tax. |
| Decision Latency | 2x slower than company avg | Operational Bottleneck: Remove/Fix approval chains. |
| Loyalty Failure | Team stays but won't follow mgr | Attrition Risk: Targeted intervention for high-talent clusters. |

## Synthesis Protocols
- **Step 1 (The Multiplier Audit):** Numeric measure of team output change due to manager presence.
- **Step 2 (The Friction Audit):** Identify top 2 "Managerial Bottlenecks" (Indecision/Review).
- **Step 3 (The Shield Audit):** Quantify "Shield Integrity" (Filtering Exec noise vs. forwarding panic).
- **Step 4 (The Blind Spot):** Identity top 3 gaps between perception and reality.

## Reporting Artifacts
**Part 1: Study Parameters** (Span of control, Team KPI, Trigger context).
**Part 2: The Multiplier Score** (Overall effectiveness in unlocking team productivity).
**Part 3: The Managerial Blind Spot Map** (Gap analysis between manager intent and report reality).
**Part 4: Shield Integrity Assessment** (Quantifying how well the team is protected from rot).
**Part 5: Actionable Coaching Roadmap** (Specify: "Enable [Behavior]" or "Cease [Friction]").
