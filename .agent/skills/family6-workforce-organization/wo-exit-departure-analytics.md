name: Exit & Departure Research (Analytics)
description: Analysis of drivers of attrition and final employee sentiment outcomes.
id: wo-exit-departure-analytics
---

<methodology>
1. **The Retention Delta**: Analyze the alignment between the company's intended retention and the employee's reported push/pull factors.
2. **Behavioral Attribution**: Distinguish between "Functional Failures" (role, team, compensation) and "Organizational Failures" (culture, manager relationship).
3. **Sentiment Consistency Correlation**: Analyze how the consistency across touchpoints (Relational vs. Functional) correlates with the likelihood to re-engage with the brand.
4. **Internal Hero Identification**: Pinpointing specific moments or people that drove the highest positive sentiment or safety feeling.
</methodology>

<insight_extraction>
- **The Departure Highlight**: What was the most successful part of the exit experience?
- **The Retention Barrier**: Identification of any major points of failure or friction in the employee path.
- **Brand Status Mastery Score**: Overall qualitative grade of the company's readiness to deliver a premium identity.
- **Actionable Strategic Plan**: Identification of priority focus areas for future retention adjustment or cultural refinement.
</insight_extraction>
