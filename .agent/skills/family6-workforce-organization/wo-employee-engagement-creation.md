---
name: Employee Engagement (Creation)
description: Briefing and methodology for psychological relationship with work and team.
id: wo-employee-engagement-creation
---

<methodology>
1. **Psychological Safety Anchor**: Ensure the brief prioritizes the feeling of safety and openness within the team environment.
2. **Work-Life Harmony Delta**: Instruction to identify the "Delta" between the employee's intended work-pacing and the actual daily pressure.
3. **Purpose & Alignment Mapping**: Determine if the employee's personal goals are perceived as aligned with the company's mission.
</methodology>

<coverage_tree>
- **Daily Morale & Energy**: The average level of enthusiasm and focus for the work.
- **Team Social Cohesion**: Relationship with peers and sense of "Having each other's back."
- **Work-Load Perception**: Fairness and sustainability of the current pressure levels.
- **Career Growth Awareness**: Clarity and accessibility of developmental opportunities.
- **Brand Pride**: How the internal brand is perceived and represented by the employee.
- **Recognition & Value Feeling**: Sense of Being valued for one's contribution.
- **Retention Efficacy**: Likelihood of the employee staying with the company for the foreseeable future.
</coverage_tree>
