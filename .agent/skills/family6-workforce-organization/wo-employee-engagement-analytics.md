## Interpretation Framework
**Goal:** Audit the "Operational Energy Exchange." Distinguish between "Active Compliance" and "Discretionary Effort."
**Key Metric:** **The Motivation Delta.** The gap between "Claimed Purpose" and "Lived Reality."
**Principle:** **Institutional Molasses.** High weight (1.4x) on descriptions of processes that "drain" energy.
**Quality Rule:** **Cynicism Multiplier.** Downweight (0.65x) respondents who recite values without behavioral anecdotes.

## Critical Misreadings
- **The Happiness Fallacy:** Mistaking "I like my coworkers" for functional engagement or accountability.
- **High-Volume Error:** Misreading extra hours as dedication rather than "Burnout/Process Disease."
- **Silence Myth:** Treating zero complaints as health; in engagement, silence often indicates "Apathy."
- **Honeymoon Bias:** Overweighting sentiment from < 6mo tenure respondents (Cap at 0.70 confidence).

## Synthesis Protocols
- **Step 1 (The Energy Audit):** Numeric delta between potential and applied energy.
- **Step 2 (The Friction Audit):** Identify top 3 "drain" processes (The Molasses Report).
- **Step 3 (The Trust Audit):** Link disengagement to specific leadership misalignment (Vision vs. Floor).
- **Step 4 (The Fix Protocol):** Recommend: "Remove X blocker to unlock Y energy."

## Reporting Artifacts
**Part 1: Study Parameters** (Demographics, Business trigger, Attrition context).
**Part 2: Executive Summary** (The Energy Gap Score and attrition risk classification).
**Part 3: The Molasses Report** (Top 3 processes/tools actively draining the team).
**Part 4: The Trust Audit** (Leadership alignment delta and communication noise/vacuum).
**Part 5: Decision Map Response** (Stretch goals for high momentum vs. work cancellation for burnout).
