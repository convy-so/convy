---
name: Employee Engagement (Analytics)
description: Analysis of employee engagement and internal brand outcomes.
id: wo-employee-engagement-analytics
---

<methodology>
1. **The Work-Harmony Delta**: Analyze the alignment between the employee's intended "Life Balance" and their reported daily pressure.
2. **Behavioral Attribution**: Distinguish between "Functional Failures" (work-load, recognition) and "Cultural Failures" (team social cohesion, lack of belonging).
3. **Engagement Persistence Correlation**: Analyze how the consistency across touchpoints (Relational vs. Functional) correlates with the likelihood to stay with the company.
4. **Internal Hero Identification**: Pinpointing specific team behaviors or recognitions that drove the highest positive sentiment.
</methodology>

<insight_extraction>
- **The Engagement Highlight**: What was the most successful part of the employee experience?
- **The Internal Barrier**: Identification of any major points of failure or friction in the workplace path.
- **Brand Pride Score**: Overall qualitative grade of the company's readiness to deliver a premium internal identity.
- **Actionable Service Plan**: Identification of priority focus areas for future team building or work-pacing refinement.
</insight_extraction>
