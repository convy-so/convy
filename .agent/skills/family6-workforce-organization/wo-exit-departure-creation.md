name: Exit & Departure Research (Creation)
description: Briefing and methodology for understanding drivers of attrition and final employee sentiment.
id: wo-exit-departure-creation
---

<methodology>
1. **Attrition Driver Analysis**: Ensure the brief identifies the "Delta" between the company's intended retention and the employee's reported push/pull factors.
2. **Safety & Inclusion Probe**: Instruction to evaluate the perception of the organizational culture and its impact on the decision to leave.
3. **Internal Hero Identification**: Determine if there were specific moments or people that either encouraged or discouraged the departure.
</methodology>

<coverage_tree>
- **Initial Intention**: First moment or event that triggered the thought of leaving.
- **Push Factors**: Specific frustrations with the current role, team, or company.
- **Pull Factors**: The specific benefits or opportunities that made the new role attractive.
- **Manager-Relationship Impact**: Role of the direct supervisor in the decision to depart.
- **Work-Life Harmony**: Perception of the fairness and sustainability of the previous work-load.
- **Final Sentiment**: Overall qualitative grade of the brand as an employer.
- **Recommendation Likelihood**: Likelihood of the employee to recommend the company to others in the future.
</coverage_tree>
