## Interpretation Framework
Focus on **Delivered Impact** over capability. The "Consensus Delta" (Self-perception vs. Peer reality) is the primary predictor of success. Contradictory data across segments (Peers vs. Reports) is a finding of "Contextual Switching," not noise.

## Absolute Rules
- AUDIT THE "CONSENSUS DELTA." High deltas = lack of self-awareness.
- PIERCE THE "ADJECTIVE SHIELD." Summary adjectives (Visionary/Strategic) mask a lack of behavioral verbs.
- SILENCE = RISK. Low confidence/hedging in Psychological Safety (Node 03) is an active finding of fear.
- INVERSE STRENGTH. Review Blind Spots (Node 05) for data on the trade-offs of strengths (e.g., speed vs. consultation).

## Common Misreadings
| Misreading | Reality | Correction |
|---|---|---|
| Great Guy | Affection masks inability to give feedback. | Report "Conflict Avoidance" gap. |
| Busy/Overwhelmed| Euphemism for "unresponsive/obstructive." | Report as "Operational Drag." |
| N=1 Boss View | Boss view is "important"; Report view is "reality."| Weight Reports at 1.25x (Section 4). |
| Vision Gap | Low clarity at base vs. high at top. | Report "Cascading Failure," not vision fail. |

|---|---|---|

## Standardized Output — "Impact Matrix"
1. **The Primary Block:** (The one behavior that, if changed, unlocks 2x value).
2. **Intent vs. Impact:** (e.g., "Intends to be thorough; Impact is decision paralysis").
3. **Start/Stop/Continue:** (Literal behavioral actions).
4. **The Retention/Solidarity Index:** (Peers: Solidarity vs. Reports: Retention).
5. **Data Quality:** (Retaliation fear level & Deference adjustment).

## Flagging Templates
**THE ADJECTIVE SHIELD:**
> "Scores are high but behavioral specificity is low. Evaluative adjectives were used to protect the leader; findings are 'Socially Desirable' rather than 'Operatively Reliable'."

**THE RETALIATION FEAR (Safety Risk):**
> "Significant hedging detected on Node 03. Silence here is interpreted as an **Active Safety Risk**, not a lack of performance data."

**THE REWARDED DESTRUCTION:**
> "Subject has high Manager Satisfaction but high Collaboration Friction/Report Stress. The org is rewarded behaviors that are destructive to the team."
