---
name: 360-Degree Feedback (Analytics)
description: Analysis of multi-rater feedback for individual growth and performance outcomes.
id: wo-360-feedback-analytics
---

<methodology>
1. **The Individual Growth Delta**: Analyze the alignment between the respondent's intended skills and their reported performance post-feedback.
2. **Behavioral Attribution**: Distinguish between "Technical Success" (technical skills) and "Interpersonal Success" (communication, collaboration).
3. **Internal Persistence Correlation**: Analyze how the consistency across touchpoints (Relational vs. Functional) correlates with the likelihood to become a recurring client.
4. **Behavioral Shift Identification**: Pinpointing specific growth areas or behaviors that drove the highest positive sentiment.
</methodology>

<insight_extraction>
- **The Performance Highlight**: What was the most successful part of the individual performance session?
- **The Self-Awareness Barrier**: Identification of any major points of failure or friction in the self-assessment.
- **Competency Growth Score**: Overall qualitative grade of the respondent's readiness to deliver a premium identity.
- **Actionable Growth Plan**: Identification of priority focus areas for future development or growth-mindset refinement.
</insight_extraction>
