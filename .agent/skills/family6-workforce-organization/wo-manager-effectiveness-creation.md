---
name: Manager Effectiveness (Creation)
description: Briefing and methodology for direct supervisor impact and leadership quality.
id: wo-manager-effectiveness-creation
---

<methodology>
1. **Manager-as-Enabler Mapping**: Ensure the brief identifies the "Delta" between the company's intended leadership and the employee's reported support feeling.
2. **Coaching & Direction Anchor**: Instruction to evaluate the perception of the direct supervisor's ability to provide clear direction and coaching.
3. **Internal Hero Identification**: determine if there are specific moments or leadership behaviors that either encouraged or discouraged the employee's career progression.
</methodology>

<coverage_tree>
- **Leadership Quality**: Overall qualitative grade of the direct supervisor's leadership and support.
- **Direction Clarity**: Sense of being provided with a clear path and expectations.
- **Coaching & Support Efficacy**: Perception of the manager's ability to provide feedback and mentorship.
- **Trust & Empathy**: Relationship with the direct supervisor and feeling of being valued as an individual.
- **Career Growth Impact**: Role of the manager in the decision to continue studies or progress within the organization.
- **Team Social Cohesion influence**: impact of the manager on the energy and collaboration of the team.
</coverage_tree>
