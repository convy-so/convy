## Interpretation Framework
**Goal:** Audit the "Structural Outcome" of meritocracy. Prioritize "Structural Access" (Power/Projects/Promotions) over "Relational Sentiment" (Liking).
**Key Metric:** **The Equity Delta.** Gap between "DEI Branding" and "Lived Structural Reality."
**Principle:** **The Representation Premium.** Upweight feedback from historically excluded segments by 1.3x on barriers.
**Quality Rule:** **The PR Penalty.** Downweight (0.70x) global positive jargon ("we're family," "colorblind meritocracy").

## Critical Misreadings
- **The Happy Token Trap:** Confusing a single successful outlier with a healthy, equitable system.
- **Culture Fit Euphemism:** Misinterpreting rejection as "fit" rather than "Unconscious Exclusion."
- **Sentiment Normalization:** Ignoring that a "3/5" from a minority segment often hides a "1/5" due to fear.
- **Honeymoon Bias:** Overweighting sentiment from < 1yr tenure respondents (Cap at 0.70 confidence).

## Proxy Metrics for Equity
| Metric | Signal | Interpretation |
|---|---|---|

| Room Simulation Voice| Who speaks in decision moments | **Voice Density.** Measures true inclusion vs. presence. |
| Mentorship vs. Promo | Mentored but never advanced | **The Coaching Trap.** Performance bias in talent mgmt. |
| Retention Velocity | Excluded quitting 2x faster | **Talent-Leaking Culture.** Structural exclusion proof. |

## Synthesis & Reporting Patterns
| Pattern | Signal | Action |
|---|---|---|
| The Unwritten Tax | Same barrier cited by 40% | Confirmed Structural Flaw: Deploy targeted intervention. |
| Authenticity Gap | Mgmt perception 180° from floor| Leadership Signal Failure: Bridge to Management Effect. |
| Silence Penalty | High "Risk Calculation" noted | Information Risk: Psych safety floor has collapsed. |
| Survival Present. | 80%+ corporate jargon/PE mask | Low Confidence: Respondent actively protecting themselves. |

## Synthesis Protocols
- **Step 1 (The Power Audit):** Narrative or visual map of high-stakes project distribution (The Power Map).
- **Step 2 (The Access Audit):** Identify the sponsorship gap (Advice vs. Advocacy).
- **Step 3 (The Tax Audit):** Quantify the "Authenticity Tax" energy drain percentage.
- **Step 4 (The Intervention):** Recommend: "Structuralize Sponsorship" or "Remove X Bypass."

## Reporting Artifacts
**Part 1: Study Parameters** (Demographics, Identity segments, Business trigger).
**Part 2: Executive Equity Summary** (Name the single most significant structural barrier to advancement).
**Part 3: The Power Map** (Analysis of visibility and high-stakes work distribution).
**Part 4: The Inclusion Gap** (Delta between PR branding and lived structural reality).
**Part 5: Decision Map Response** (Formal sponsorship programs vs. project reallocation).

## Flagging Templates
**SURVIVAL PRESENTATION:**
> "High defensive posturing. 80%+ corporate jargon/PR Mask. zero behavioral anecdotes. Feedback is a 'Survival Presentation'. Low diagnostic confidence."

**PERFORMATIVE EQUITY:**
> "Leadership perception of DEI is 180-degrees from structural reality. 'Authenticity Gap' detected. High risk of imminent excluded talent attrition."

**TALENT LEAK:**
> "Excluded segments quitting 2x faster than majority. Structural exclusion from 'Golden Project' pipeline confirmed. High organizational expertise loss."
