---
name: DEI Experience Research (Analytics)
description: Analysis of perception of diversity, equity, and inclusion in the workplace outcomes.
id: wo-dei-experience-analytics
---

<methodology>
1. **The Inclusion Delta**: Analyze the alignment between the company's intended "Safe Place" and the employee's reported inclusive feeling post-discussion.
2. **Behavioral Attribution**: Distinguish between "Functional Failures" (equity, DEI practices) and "Cultural Failures" (lack of belonging, inclusion).
3. **Internal Brand Consistency Correlation**: Analyze how the consistency across touchpoints (Relational vs. Functional) correlates with the likelihood to stick with the organization.
4. **Internal Hero Identification**: Pinpointing specific DEI-related behaviors or recognitions that drove the highest positive sentiment.
</methodology>

<insight_extraction>
- **The DEI Success Highlight**: What was the most successful part of the DEI experience?
- **The Inclusive Barrier**: Identification of any major points of failure or friction in the DEI framework.
- **Brand Status Mastery Score**: Overall qualitative grade of the company's readiness to deliver a premium internal identity.
- **Actionable Strategic Plan**: Identification of priority focus areas for future DEI refinement or culture building.
</insight_extraction>
