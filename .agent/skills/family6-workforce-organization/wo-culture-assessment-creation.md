---
name: Culture & Values Assessment (Creation)
description: Briefing and methodology for measuring lived company values and organizational norms.
id: wo-culture-assessment-creation
---

<methodology>
1. **Lived-Value Mapping**: Ensure the brief identifies the "Delta" between the company's stated values and the employee's reported lived experience.
2. **Norms & Behavioral Anchor**: Instruction to evaluate the perception of the organizational habits and its impact on the energy of the team.
3. **Internal Brand Consistency**: Determine if there are specific patterns or associations that reflect an underlying belief in the company's mission or brand.
</methodology>

<coverage_tree>
- **Implicit Associations**: The first word or feeling that comes to mind when the company is mentioned.
- **Lived Values**: How well the company's stated values are perceived to be lived by the employees and leadership.
- **Organizational Norms**: The common behaviors and habits that define the team Energy.
- **Leadership Alignment**: How well the leadership's actions are perceived to reflect the company's mission.
- **Sense of Belonging**: Overall feeling of being valued and heard within the organizational culture.
- **Brand Reputation**: How the internal brand is perceived and represented by the employee.
</coverage_tree>
