## Interpretation Framework
Prioritize **Internal Push Factors** (exhaustion, toxicity, lack of growth) over **External Pull Factors** (better salary, title). The goal is to map the "Retention Failure Point"—the precise structural friction that made the employee receptive to external offers.

## Absolute Rules
- ANCHOR ON THE CATALYST. Identify the "Inciting Incident" (The Snap) to separate "General Morale" from the "Final Straw."
- EXPOSE "Polite Fictions." "Better Opportunity" usually means "No path upward here."
- AUDIT "The Ghost Ship." Identify specific operational/technical risks left behind by the departure.
- MEASURE "Prevention Gap." Was the issue flagged to leadership 3+ months before resignation? (Node 04).

## Common Misreadings
| Misreading | Reality | Correction |
|---|---|---|
| Better Opp. | "This company stopped offering a path." | Report sub-node: "Career Growth Stagnation." |
| Money pull | Money justifications for leaving a drain. | Re-weight to internal "Misery Index." |
| Manager Halo | Liked by team, but seniors are quitting. | Review attrition velocity by seniority. |
| Performance | "Everything was fine." (Notice period mask) | Reference Paranoia adjustment (0.65x). |

## Node Interpretation & Weights
| Node | Focus | Indicator | Weighting |
|---|---|---|---|
| EXIT-01 | Catalyst | Chronic vs. Discrete incident details. | 1.5x (Critical) |
| EXIT-02 | Reason Delta| Official vs. Forensic reason gap. | 1.3x (Critical) |
| EXIT-03 | Manager | Specific behavioral failure (not personality). | 1.0x |
| EXIT-05 | Ghost Ship | Unseen labor/Technical knowledge loss. | 1.2x (Urgent) |

## Quality Weighting (v1.1.0)
| Condition | Rule | Penalty/Boost |
|---|---|---|
| Ref. Paranoia | Global praise while still in notice period. | -0.35 Penalty (0.65x) |
| Snapshot | Specific, chronological anecdote of a trust break. | +0.50 Boost (1.5x) |
| Reality Check | Counter-offer rejection logic. | +0.20 Boost (High Confidence). |
| Performance | Mentions corporate jargon/PR phrases. | Cap Confidence at 0.70. |

## Standardized Output — "Post-Mortem Audit"
1. **The Real Reason:** (Stripping away the "Polite Fiction").
2. **The Attrition Catalyst:** (The single event that broke internal loyalty).
3. **The Ghost Ship Risk:** (Immediate operational red-alerts).
4. **Managerial Blind Spot:** (Index of manager "surprise" vs. "warning").
5. **Contagion Forecast:** (Risk to remaining high-value talent).

## Flagging Templates
**THE REFERENCE PARANOIA:**
> "Respondent uses consistent positive generalizations and jargon while avoiding specific anecdotes. Indicates high fear of reference sabotage; findings likely contain 'Polite Fictions'."

**THE EUPHORIC VENTER:**
> "Respondent is in the immediate aftermath of a high-pressure exit. Anecdotes are specific but emotionally charged. Cross-reference with 'Friction Tax' of remaining team."

**THE IGNORED WARNING (Preventability):**
> "The leaver flagged this issue to leadership at least 3 months prior to exodus. This exit is categorized as **Structurally Preventable (Leadership Failure)**."
