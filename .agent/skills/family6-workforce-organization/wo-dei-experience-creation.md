---
name: DEI Experience Research (Creation)
description: Briefing and methodology for perception of diversity, equity, and inclusion in the workplace.
id: wo-dei-experience-creation
---

<methodology>
1. **Psychological Safety Anchor**: Ensure the brief clearly identifies the degree of safety and welcome felt by employees of all backgrounds.
2. **Inclusion & Belonging Delta**: Instruction to evaluate the perception of the organizational culture and its impact on the energy of the team.
3. **Internal Brand Consistency**: Determine if there are specific patterns or associations that reflect an underlying belief in the company's commitment to DEI.
</methodology>

<coverage_tree>
- **Implicit Associations**: The first word or feeling that comes to mind when the company's DEI culture is mentioned.
- **Perception of Inclusion**: How well the company's stated DEI values are perceived to be lived by the employees and leadership.
- **Diversity Awareness**: The average level of recognition for the importance of diversity in the core mission.
- **Equity Perception**: Fairness and sustainability of the current DEI practices.
- **Sense of Belonging**: Overall feeling of being valued and heard within the DEI framework.
- **Brand Reputation**: How the internal DEI brand is perceived and represented by the employee.
</coverage_tree>
