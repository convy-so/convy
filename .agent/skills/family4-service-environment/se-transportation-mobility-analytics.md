<methodology>
1. **The Reliability Foundation**: Analyze whether the core promise of getting from Point A to Point B on time was met, evaluating all other experience dimensions through this lens.
2. **Behavioral Attribution**: Distinguish between "Service Failures" (staff rudeness) and "Systemic Failures" (chronic delays, poor signage).
3. **Disruption Tolerance**: Analyze how proactive communication correlates with the respondent's willingness to forgive a delay.
4. **Touchpoint Friction Analysis**: Identification of specific moments (e.g., boarding gate vs. baggage claim) that create the most passenger anxiety.
</methodology>

<insight_extraction>
- **The Transit Highlight**: What was the most successful or comfortable part of the journey?
- **The Friction Point**: Identification of any major points of anxiety or failure in the passenger flow.
- **Reliability-Versus-Experience Score**: Overall qualitative grade comparing the operational execution against the customer service delivery.
- **Actionable Operational Plan**: Identification of priority focus areas for future process or communication adjustment.
</insight_extraction>
