---
name: Hospitality Experience (Analytics)
description: Analysis of guest journey and hospitality experience outcomes.
id: se-hospitality-experience-analytics
---

<methodology>
1. **The Guest Emotion Delta**: Analyze the alignment between the property's intended "Welcome" feeling and the guest's reported emotion post-stay.
2. **Behavioral Attribution**: Distinguish between "Physical Failures" (room quality, amenities) and "Service Failures" (staff interaction, process errors).
3. **Experience Consistency Correlation**: Analyze how the consistency across touchpoints (Check-in, Dining, Checkout) correlates with the overall likelihood to re-book.
4. **Service Hero Identification**: Pinpointing specific staff roles or behaviors that drove the highest positive sentiment.
</methodology>

<insight_extraction>
- **The Stay Highlight**: What was the single most successful part of the hospitality experience?
- **The Journey Barrier**: Identification of any major points of failure or friction in the guest path.
- **Brand Hospitality Mastery**: Overall qualitative grade of the property's readiness to deliver a premium guest experience.
- **Actionable Service Plan**: Identification of priority focus areas for future staff training or amenity adjustment.
</insight_extraction>
