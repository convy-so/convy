## Interpretation Framework
**Goal:** Distinguish between performer-driven euphoria and venue-driven logistics.
**Principle:** The Energy Arc. Managing the spatial/sensory journey from Intake to Egress.
**Tribal Halo:** Blindness to failure. A fan’s love for the team/artist causes them to forgive dangerous or incompetent venue ops.
**Loss of Action:** Operational bottlenecks (merch/F&B lines) that cost the fan show time.
**TV Substitute Threat:** Logistical pain (egress/parking) driving fans toward at-home viewing.

## Critical Misreadings
- **The Euphoric 10/10:** If the team won, the fan will report 10/10 venue satisfaction regardless of dirty bathrooms. Use the "Separation Probe" results to isolate the building.
- **Crowd Energy vs. Safety:** High energy is not a proxy for high safety. Look for mentions of "trapped" or "squeezed" (SE-03) even in positive reviews.
- **Recency Effect (Egress):** A perfect 3-hour show is permanently poisoned by a 2-hour parking lot jam. Weight Egress (SE-05) heavily for return intent.

## Evidence Hierarchy
| Tier | Title | Evidence Type |
|---|---|---|
| Tier 1 | "Crush" / Safety (1.00) | Critical: "I felt physically trapped at the south gate during entry." |
| Tier 2 | Loss of Action (0.95) | Specific: "Line was so slow I missed the entire first quarter." |
| Tier 3 | Egress Friction (0.85) | Exit Fail: "No one directing traffic; sat in parking lot for 90 mins." |
| Tier 4 | Sightline/Sound (0.60) | Sensory: "Pole blocked half the stage" or "Sound was muffled." |
| Tier 5 | Generic Vibe (0.35) | Noise: "Great energy" / "Awesome show" (Weighted 0.65; contextual). |

## Synthesis & Reporting Patterns
| Pattern | Signal | Action |
|---|---|---|
| The Loyal Sufferer | High Performer Love / Low Venue Logistics | Churn risk if performer quality slips; venue is a liability. |
| The Logistical Burnout | High Logistics Pain / Low Return Intent | The "Never Again" fan. Exit friction outweighed the show. |
| The TV Substitute | High Cost/Hassle / Tech Comparison | Fan prefers the 4K couch; venue hasn't justified the ticket. |
| The Halo Mismatch | Team Win / Facility Failure | Structural operational gaps being masked by tribal success. |

## Scoring Weighting
- **Intensity Multiplier (x0.5 on Halo):** Discount sentiment if only Tier 5 "Euphoria" is present without Tier 4 "Facility" details.
- **Egress Discount (-0.20):** Subtract from overall sentiment if Tier 3 evidence (Egress) exceeds 45 mins.

## Reporting Artifacts
**Part 1: Executive Summary** (The Flow vs. Euphoria Balance).
**Part 2: Structural Findings** (Concourse Choke-points & Sightline Failures).
**Part 3: Operational Risk** (Safety Signals & Egress Exhaustion).
**Part 4: Recommendations** (Wayfinding vs. Staffing vs. Infrastructure).

## Escalation Protocols
- **Physical Safety/Crush:** Reports of feeling trapped, squeezed, or violence in the stands.
- **Systemic Abandonment:** No police/staff directing mass crowds during Egress or Emergencies.

## Reporting Flag Templates
**TRIBAL HALO:**
> "The data exhibits a strong 'Tribal Halo.' High overall sentiment is driven by Performer Euphoria (SE-04) rather than Venue Logistics. However, Tier 2 evidence regarding 'Loss of Action' suggests operational gaps that will drive churn during a losing season."

**LOGISTICAL BURNOUT:**
> "The respondent reports 'Logistical Burnout.' While the core event was high quality, the Tier 3 friction (Egress/Parking) has permanently damaged 'Return Intent.' The hassle currently outweighs the reward."
