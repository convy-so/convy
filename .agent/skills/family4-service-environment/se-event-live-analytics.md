name: Event & Live Experience (Analytics)
description: Analysis of concert, sports, and live show experience outcomes.

---

<methodology>
1. **Peak-End Memory Delta**: Analyze the alignment between the event's intended emotional impact and the respondent's reported memory of their highest-intensity moment and departure.
2. **Behavioral Attribution**: Distinguish between "Logistical Failures" (long lines, bad sound) and "Content Failures" (the performance was boring).
3. **Crowd Effect Correlation**: Analyze how the perception of other attendees' behavior correlates with the respondent's overall sense of safety and enjoyment.
4. **Anticipation-Delivery Analysis**: Identification of areas where pre-event marketing overly inflated expectations versus actual delivery.
</methodology>

<insight_extraction>
- **The Peak Experience**: What was the most successful and emotionally resonant part of the event?
- **The Logistical Barrier**: Identification of any major points of friction in the venue operations.
- **Atmosphere Mastery Score**: Overall qualitative grade of the event's ability to cultivate shared energy.
- **Actionable Production Plan**: Identification of priority focus areas for future event flow or facility management.
</insight_extraction>
