name: Personal Care & Wellness (Analytics)
description: Analysis of salon, spa, gym, and clinic experience outcomes.
id: se-personal-care-analytics
---

<methodology>
1. **The Individual Transformation Delta**: Analyze the alignment between the client's intended "Wellness Goal" and their reported outcome post-treatment.
2. **Behavioral Attribution**: Distinguish between "Technical Success" (haircut was good) and "Experience Success" (I felt relaxed, I felt heard).
3. **Practitioner Trust Correlation**: Analyze how the consistency across touchpoints (Relational vs. Functional) correlates with the likelihood to become a recurring client.
4. **Environment Impact Analysis**: Pinpointing specific physical or sensory elements that enhanced or distracted from the wellness experience.
</methodology>

<insight_extraction>
- **The Treatment Highlight**: What was the most successful part of the personal care session?
- **The Practitioner Barrier**: Identification of any major points of failure or friction in the practitioner relationship.
- **Facility Excellence Score**: Overall qualitative grade of the environment's readiness to deliver a premium wellness experience.
- **Actionable Strategic Plan**: Identification of priority focus areas for future staff training or amenity adjustment.
</insight_extraction>
