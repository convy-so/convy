name: Retail & In-Store Experience (Analytics)

---

<methodology>
1. **The Spatial Journey Delta**: Analyze the alignment between the store's intended layout and the shopper's reported discovery success.
2. **Behavioral Attribution**: Distinguish between "Product Failures" (out of stock) and "Experience Failures" (couldn't find it, staff was unhelpful).
3. **Ambiance-Wait Correlation**: Analyze how the store's environment (music, lighting) correlates with the respondent's tolerance for checkout wait times.
4. **Staff Efficacy Analysis**: Identification of small, recurring annoyances that indicate a failure in the long-term sales or service training.
</methodology>

<insight_extraction>
- **The Store Highlight**: What was the most successful part of the physical shopping experience?
- **The Navigation Barrier**: Identification of any major points of failure or confusion in the spatial path.
- **Trial-Confidence Score**: Overall qualitative grade of the environment's readiness to support purchase decisions.
- **Actionable Retail Plan**: Identification of priority focus areas for future layout or staff training adjustment.
</insight_extraction>
