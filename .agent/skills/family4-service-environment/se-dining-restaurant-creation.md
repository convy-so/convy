<methodology>
1. **The Holistic Experience**: Food, Service, and Ambiance are interdependent. The brief must instruction to isolate each dimension.
2. **Context Elicitation**: Identify the "Occasion" (Celebration, Business, Quick bite) as it anchors expectations.
3. **Menu Coverage**: Ensure the sampling plan covers specific dishes (Appetizers, Mains, Drinks).
</methodology>

<coverage_tree>
- **The Visit**: Date, time, and party size.
- **Service Interaction**: Reservation ease, seating warmth, server knowledge, and speed.
- **The Product**: Taste, temperature, and presentation of specific items ordered.
- **Environmental Context**: Noise level, music selection, lighting, and seating comfort.
- **Value Perception**: Perceived ROI of the final bill.
- **Future Intent**: Return propensity and recommendation likelihood.
</coverage_tree>
