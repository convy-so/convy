name: Personal Care & Wellness (Creation)
description: Briefing and methodology for salon, spa, gym, and clinic experience research.
id: se-personal-care-creation
---

<methodology>
1. **Relational Trust**: Ensure the brief identifies the importance of the practitioner-client relationship (Technical vs. Interpersonal).
2. **Physical Sensitivity**: Instruction to evaluate the environment's cleanliness, comfort, and sense of "Safe Space."
3. **Outcome Perception**: Identify if the primary goal is a "Result" (Haircut, Training) or an "Experience" (Relaxation, Wellness).
</methodology>

<coverage_tree>
- **Initial Atmosphere**: Smell, lighting, and "sense of being cared for" upon arrival.
- **Practitioner Interaction**: Professionalism, empathy, and technical expertise of the staff.
- **Facility Quality**: Equipment hygiene, material comfort, and ambiance.
- **The Treatment Experience**: Specific feedback on the technical service delivered.
- **Post-Service Feeling**: Immediate and long-term satisfaction with the result.
- **Value Recognition**: Perception of trade-off between price and personal transformation.
</coverage_tree>
