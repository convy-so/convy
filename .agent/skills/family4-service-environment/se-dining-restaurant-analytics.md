<methodology>
1. **Dimension Weighting**: Analyze whether the overall satisfaction was driven primarily by the Food quality, the Service warmth, or the Ambiance.
2. **The Hospitality Gap**: Identify the delta between "Technical Service" (taking orders) and "Interpersonal Hospitality" (warmth, initiative).
3. **Sentiment Intersection**: Correlate sensory descriptors (e.g., "over-salted," "vibrant") with overall value-for-money perception.
</methodology>

<insight_extraction>
- **The Signature Strength**: What is the restaurant's standout dimension?
- **The Friction Root**: Is the primary issue Operational (speed), Culinary (taste), or Ambient (comfort)?
- **Server Performance Analysis**: Analyze the behaviors that drove positive or negative sentiment.
- **Actionable Menu Insights**: Flag specific dishes that are underperforming or driving consistent praise.
</insight_extraction>
