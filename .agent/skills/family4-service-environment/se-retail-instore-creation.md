<methodology>
1. **Spatial Journey Flow**: Ensure the brief identifies the sequence from entering the store to discovery to checkout.
2. **Product Discovery Effectiveness**: Instruction to evaluate how easy it was to find specific items or be inspired by the layout.
3. **Staff Intervention Quality**: Identify if staff were perceived as "helpful assistants" or "intrusive sales-drivers."
</methodology>

<coverage_tree>
- **First Impression**: Ambiance, music, lighting, and "sense of being welcomed."
- **Layout & Navigation**: Ease of move-through and logical grouping of products.
- **Product Availability**: Success in finding desired items or relevant alternatives.
- **Staff Interaction**: Quality of assistance and interpersonal warmth.
- **Trial & Testing Experience**: Comfort and functionality of fitting rooms or demo areas.
- **Checkout Efficiency**: Speed, ease of payment, and the final impression at departure.
</coverage_tree>
