---
name: Hospitality Experience (Creation)
description: Briefing and methodology for guest journey research at hotels and resorts.
id: se-hospitality-experience-creation
---

<methodology>
1. **Multi-Touchpoint Journey**: Ensure the brief covers the full arc from booking to checkout and post-stay feeling.
2. **Expectation Hierarchy**: Identify if the primary goal is "Reliability/Comfort" (Business) or "Experience/Surprise" (Leisure).
3. **Staff Interaction Intensity**: Instruction to evaluate the warmth and effectiveness of interpersonal touchpoints (Concierge, Housekeeping, Front Desk).
</methodology>

<coverage_tree>
- **Booking & Arrival**: Reservation ease and the first "Check-in" impression.
- **Room Quality**: Cleanliness, functional comfort (bed/shower), and ambiance.
- **Amenities & Facilities**: Usage and satisfaction with gym, pool, or business center.
- **Food & Beverage**: On-site dining and room service quality.
- **Staff Service**: Professionalism and interpersonal warmth.
- **Value for Money**: Perception of trade-off between price and experience.
- **Departure & Post-Stay**: Checkout efficiency and overall memory of the stay.
</coverage_tree>
