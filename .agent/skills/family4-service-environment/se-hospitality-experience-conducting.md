<probe_library>
- **The Arrival Elicitation**: "Thinking back to when you first arrived at the [Property], how would you describe the moment you walked through the door? What was the first signal that you were in good hands?"
- **Room Quality Probe**: "How did the room match your expectation for [Purpose of Stay]? What was the specific detail that felt either like a thoughtful touch or a missed opportunity?"
- **Interpersonal Warmth**: "Tell me about your interaction with the [Staff Role]. How did they make you feel as a guest? Was there a moment where they went above and beyond?"
- **The Departure Reflection**: "As you were checking out, what was the overarching feeling you had about your visit? What would have made the stay perfect?"
</probe_library>
