name: Transportation & Mobility (Creation)
description: Briefing and methodology for point-to-point journey experience across transport modes.
id: se-transportation-mobility-creation
---

<methodology>
1. **Reliability Supremacy**: Ensure the brief clearly isolates the core promise (did it leave and arrive on time?) from the experience dimensions (comfort, service).
2. **Journey Segmentation**: Break the experience into Booking, Pre-boarding/Station, Vehicle Experience, and Arrival.
3. **Disruption Assessment**: If a delay or issue occurred, heavily weight the research toward how the disruption was communicated and managed.
</methodology>

<coverage_tree>
- **Booking & Ticketing**: Ease of purchase, clarity of fare rules, and communication prior to travel.
- **Terminal/Station Experience**: Navigation, waiting areas, and clarity of announcements.
- **Boarding Process**: Efficiency, organization, and staff interaction during boarding.
- **In-Transit Comfort**: Seat comfort, cleanliness, temperature, and ride smoothness.
- **Onboard Service**: Quality of food/beverage and staff attentiveness (if applicable).
- **Communication & Reliability**: Timeliness of departure/arrival and transparency of any delays.
- **Arrival & Disembarkation**: Ease of leaving the vehicle and retrieving luggage.
</coverage_tree>
