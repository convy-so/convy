---
name: Software Experience Research (Analytics)
description: Analysis of daily usage and accumulated digital experience outcomes.

---

<methodology>
1. **The JTBD Alignment Delta**: Analyze the alignment between the software's intended task flow and the user's reported success post-usage period.
2. **Behavioral Attribution**: Distinguish between "Functional Failures" (system didn't work) and "Mental Model Failures" (user didn't understand how it works).
3. **Workflow Integration Score**: Metric assessment of how naturally the product fit into the user's routine without requiring excessive accommodation.
4. **Friction Accumulation Analysis**: Identification of small, recurring annoyances that indicate a failure in the long-term UI/UX.
</methodology>

<insight_extraction>
- **The Software Highlight**: What was the most successful part of the digital experience?
- **The Workflow Barrier**: Identification of any major points of failure or confusion in the primary flow.
- **Mental Model Gaps**: Analysis of why the user's expectation wasn't matched by the app's internal logic.
- **Actionable Strategic Plan**: Identification of priority focus areas for future service design or adjustment.
</insight_extraction>
