---

<methodology>
1. **The Feature Value Delta**: Analyze the alignment between the feature's intended value and the user's reported success post-usage period.
2. **Behavioral Attribution**: Distinguish between "Functional Failures" (system didn't work) and "Mental Model Failures" (user didn't understand how it works).
3. **Usage Frequency Correlation**: Analyze how feature satisfaction correlates with overall brand affinity and intended usage.
</methodology>

<insight_extraction>
- **The Feature Highlight**: What is the feature's primary functional strength?
- **The Adoption Barrier**: Identification of any major points of failure or confusion in the primary flow.
- **Mental Model Gaps**: Analysis of why the user's expectation wasn't matched by the app's internal logic.
- **Instructional Needs**: Analysis of where the user's manual help-seeking indicates a failure in self-service UI.
</insight_extraction>
