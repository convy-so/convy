---
name: Technology Adoption (Creation)
description: Briefing and methodology for measuring barriers and drivers for new technology rollout.
id: dp-technology-adoption-creation
---

<methodology>
1. **Change Psychology Grounding**: Ensure the brief identifies the "Transformation Goal" (e.g., Migration to cloud, new ERP rollout).
2. **Resistance Identification**: Instruction to distinguish between technical barriers (UI/UX) and cultural barriers (resigned to old ways).
3. **Workflow Disruption**: Core focus on where the new technology breaks existing processes or habits.
</methodology>

<coverage_tree>
- **Awareness & Readiness**: Initial understanding of the need for the new technology.
- **The Learning Curve**: Ease of configuration, training, and first-time use.
- **Activation Moment**: Success or failure of the first attempt to use the technology.
- **Support Journey**: Need for and quality of manual assistance during rollout.
- **Adoption Efficacy**: Confidence level in the technology post-onboarding.
- **Resistance Factors**: Specific reasons for potential engagement drop-off.
</coverage_tree>
