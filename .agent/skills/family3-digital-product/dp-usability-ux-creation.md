<methodology>
1. **Task-Based Grounding**: Ensure the brief clearly identifies the specific tasks to be tested (e.g., "Complete a purchase," "Change notification settings").
2. **Device Context**: Instruction to specify whether the testing is on Desktop, Mobile (App), or Tablet.
3. **Friction Identification**: Core focus on UI-level barriers (e.g., confusing navigation, hidden buttons).
</methodology>

<coverage_tree>
- **Initial Impression**: First reaction to the interface's layout and visual design.
- **Task Success**: Ability to complete the primary goal of the session.
- **Navigation Flow**: Ease of finding items and moving between pages.
- **Micro-Interactions**: Satisfaction with buttons, forms, and feedback signals.
- **Overall Usability (SUS)**: Perceived effort to use the system.
</coverage_tree>
