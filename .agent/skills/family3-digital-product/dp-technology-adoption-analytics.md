<methodology>
1. **The Adoption Delta**: Analyze the alignment between the reorganization's intended goals and the user's reported success post-rollout journey.
2. **Behavioral Attribution**: Distinguish between "Functional Failures" (system didn't work) and "Mental Model Failures" (user didn't understand how it works).
3. **Usage Frequency Correlation**: Analyze how technology satisfaction correlates with perceived productivity in deep-work versus collaborative modes.
4. **Resistance Factor Analysis**: Identification of small, recurring annoyances that indicate a failure in the long-term UI/UX.
</methodology>

<insight_extraction>
- **The Activation Hero**: What was the most successful part of the rollout setup?
- **The Adoption Killer**: Where did the most significant engagement drop-off occur?
- **Support-Trigger Analysis**: Identify common questions that indicate missing instructional content.
- **Actionable Adoption Plan**: Identification of priority focus areas for future rollout or training adjustment.
</insight_extraction>
