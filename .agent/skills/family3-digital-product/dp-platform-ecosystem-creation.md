---
name: Platform & Ecosystem (Creation)
description: Briefing and methodology for developer and partner experience with APIs and platforms.
id: dp-platform-ecosystem-creation
---

<methodology>
1. **Technical Stakeholder Alignment**: Ensure the brief identifies the specific role (Developer, Product Manager, CTO) of the respondent.
2. **Integration Friction**: Instruction to evaluate the ease of technical setup, documentation quality, and API reliability.
3. **Ecosystem Value**: Determine if the platform's community and partner benefits outweigh the technical hurdles.
</methodology>

<coverage_tree>
- **Onboarding & Setup**: Ease of getting started with API keys and documentation.
- **Documentation Quality**: Clarity, completeness, and searchability of the technical guides.
- **Technical Performance**: Perceived reliability, speed, and error-handling of the platform.
- **Support & Community**: Effectiveness of developer forums, tickets, and technical assistance.
- **Strategic Impact**: How the platform enables or hinders the partner's business goals.
- **Future Commitment**: Likelihood of the partner to continue building or integrating.
</coverage_tree>
