---
name: Platform & Ecosystem (Analytics)
description: Analysis of developer and partner experience for APIs and platforms.
id: dp-platform-ecosystem-analytics
---

<methodology>
1. **The Technical Integration Delta**: Analyze the alignment between the platform's documentation guidance and the developer's reported integration success.
2. **Behavioral Attribution**: Distinguish between "Performance Failures" (latency, downtime) and "Experience Failures" (poor documentation, confusion).
3. **Ecosystem Value Correlation**: Analyze how the partner's perceived business value of the platform correlates with their willingness to tolerate technical hurdles.
4. **Strategic Risk Assessment**: Identification of patterns that indicate high-risk competitive alternatives or developer friction.
</methodology>

<insight_extraction>
- **The Platform Highlight**: What was the most successful part of the technical experience?
- **The Integration Barrier**: Identification of any major points of failure or confusion in the technical docs.
- **Support-Efficacy Score**: Overall qualitative grade of the developer relationship health.
- **Actionable Strategic Plan**: Identification of priority focus areas for future API or documentation improvement.
</insight_extraction>
