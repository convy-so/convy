<methodology>
1. **The Task Success Delta**: Analyze the alignment between the product's intended task flow and the actual reported task success post-usage period.
2. **Interface Attribution**: Distinguish between "Functional Failures" (system didn't work) and "Mental Model Failures" (respondent didn't understand how it works).
3. **Observation-to-Insight**: Metric assessment of where navigation was the most confusing and why (e.g., iconography, labeling, hierarchy).
</methodology>

<insight_extraction>
- **The Usability Signature**: What is the most intuitive part of the interface?
- **The Navigation Barrier**: Identification of any major points of failure or confusion in the primary flow.
- **Mental Model Gaps**: Analysis of why the user's expectation didn't match the app's internal logic.
- **Instructional Needs**: Analysis of where the user's manual help-seeking indicates a failure in self-service UI.
</insight_extraction>
