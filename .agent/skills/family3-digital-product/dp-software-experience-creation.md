---
name: Software Experience Research (Creation)
description: Briefing and methodology for daily usage and accumulated experience with a digital product.
id: dp-software-experience-creation
---

<methodology>
1. **Jobs-to-be-Done (JTBD) Framing**: Ensure the brief identifies the core "Job" the user is hiring the software to do.
2. **Workflow Fit**: Instruction to evaluate how the product integrates into the user's existing habits and toolchain.
3. **Accumulated Friction**: Identify the small, recurring annoyances that diminish long-term satisfaction even if core tasks are successful.
</methodology>

<coverage_tree>
- **Daily Routine**: How the software is used throughout an average day.
- **Value Realization**: Specific moments where the software genuinely solves a business or personal problem.
- **Workflow Interruption**: Where the software's logic clashes with the user's natural mental model.
- **Emotional Relationship**: Sense of delight, frustration, or indifference toward the brand.
- **Retention Probability**: Likelihood of the user to continue usage or recommend to others.
</coverage_tree>
