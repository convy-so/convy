<methodology>
1. **Gaming-Loop Delta Mapping**: Ensure the brief identifies the "Delta" between the game's intended player motivation and the actual engagement feeling.
2. **Mechanics & Reward Anchor**: Instruction to evaluate the perception of the game's reward systems and its impact on the energy of the player persistence.
3. **Implicit Motivation Identification**: determine if there are specific narrative or mechanical signals that reflect an underlying belief in the game's value.
</methodology>

<coverage_tree>
- **Initial Impression**: First word or feeling that comes to mind when the game is mentioned.
- **Player Motivation**: Drivers of participation and engagement levels (Social, Challenge, Mastery).
- **Gaming-Loop Efficacy**: How well the game mechanics are perceived to meet the player needs.
- **Institutional Trust**: Relationship with the game development team and feeling of being valued as a player.
- **Thematic Resonance**: Sense of the game's alignment with the player's values and beliefs.
- **Reward Recognition**: perception of the trade-off between the player's time/effort and the game's rewards.
- **Persistence Likelihood**: Likelihood of the player to continue the game or recommending it to others.
</coverage_tree>
