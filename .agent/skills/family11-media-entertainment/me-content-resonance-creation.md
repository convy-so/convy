<methodology>
1. **Narrative Resonance Mapping**: Ensure the brief clearly identifies the intended "Delta" between the story's message and the audience's reported emotion.
2. **Character & Plot Efficacy**: Instruction to evaluate the perception of the story's character development and its impact on the energy of the audience interaction.
3. **Implicit Hook Identification**: Determine if there are specific narrative signals or themes that reflect an underlying belief in the story's value.
</methodology>

<coverage_tree>
- **Initial Impression**: First word or feeling that comes to mind when the content is mentioned.
- **Character Relatability**: perception of the story's character depth and its impact on the energy of the audience.
- **Plot Engagement**: How well the story's narrative is perceived to match the respondent's expectation.
- **Emotional Impact**: Overall qualitative grade of the story's readiness to deliver a premium identity.
- **Thematic Resonance**: Sense of the story's alignment with the audience's values and beliefs.
- **Brand Reputation**: How the story representing the media company brand and values.
- **Consumption Persistence Likelihood**: Likelihood of the audience to continue consuming the content or recommending it to others.
</coverage_tree>
