<probe_library>
- **The Gaming Impression Elicitation**: "Thinking back to the last week of participation in the [Game], what's the first word or feeling that comes to your mind? What was the context of that moment?"
- **Mechanics & Reward-Efficacy Probe**: "How clear and relevant do you perceive the game's reward systems to be for your player motivation? Can you walk me through a specific moment where the mechanics really stands out? Was there any reward that stood out?"
- **Institutional Trust Elicitation**: "How would you describe your relationship with the game development team? How did their influence or energy really stood out as a positive part of the study Experience? Was there a moment where their support really made a difference?"
- **The Motivation Reflection**: "As leadership moves through their work, how do they perceive the player's trust in the game? Is there a particular part of the gaming loop that contributes most to your pride in the alignment?"
</probe_library>
