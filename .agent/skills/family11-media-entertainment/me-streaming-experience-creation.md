name: Streaming & Platform Experience (Creation)
description: Briefing and methodology for usability and value perception of digital media platforms.
id: me-streaming-experience-creation
---

<methodology>
1. **Discovery & Navigation Delta**: Ensure the brief identifies the "Delta" between the platform's intended content discovery and the audience's reported ease-of-use.
2. **Subscription Value Anchor**: Instruction to evaluate the perception of the platform's price-to-value ratio and its impact on the energy of the persistence.
3. **Implicit Efficiency Mapping**: Determine if there are specific UI/UX signals or features that reflect an underlying belief in the platform's technical superiority.
</methodology>

<coverage_tree>
- **Arrival & Home Screen**: First impression of the platform brand and the relevance of the initial recommendations.
- **Search & Filter Efficacy**: Ease of locating specific content and the quality of the system's responses.
- **Discovery Experience**: Satisfaction with the platform's ability to suggest new or relevant content.
- **Streaming Quality & Reliability**: Perception of the video/audio performance and technical stability.
- **UI/UX Comfort**: Ease of navigating the interface and the clarity of the platform's logic.
- **Value Recognition**: Perception of the trade-off between the subscription cost and the total media Experience.
- **Persistence Likelihood**: Likelihood of the audience to continue the subscription or recommending it to others.
</coverage_tree>
