name: Audience Segmentation & Habits (Creation)
description: Briefing and methodology for shifting media habits across multiple channels.

---

<methodology>
1. **Consumption-Habits Delta**: Ensure the brief identifies the "Delta" between the media's intended audience reach and the respondent's reported fragmentation.
2. **Channel & Journey Anchor**: Instruction to evaluate the perception of the audience's journey across platforms and its impact on the energy of the brand interaction.
3. **Implicit Habit Mapping**: Determine if there are specific consumption signals or rituals that reflect an underlying belief in the media's value.
</methodology>

<coverage_tree>
- **Initial Awareness**: First moment or event that triggered the thought of a new media channel.
- **Consumption Habits**: Day-in-the-life mapping of media consumption across multiple channels.
- **Channel Relevancy**: Perception of the platform's ability to provide the right content at the right time.
- **Brand Story Synergy**: How well the different media channels are perceived to align on the core mission.
- **Platform Experience**: Satisfaction with the individual platform's usability and value.
- **Sense of Belonging**: Overall feeling of being valued and heard within the media ecosystem.
- **Consumption Persistence Likelihood**: Likelihood of the audience to continue the multi-channel relationship.
</coverage_tree>
