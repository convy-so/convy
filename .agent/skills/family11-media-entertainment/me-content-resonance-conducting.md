<probe_library>
- **The Content Impression Elicitation**: "Thinking back to the last time you watched [Show/Film], what's the first word or feeling that comes to your mind? What was the context of that moment?"
- **Character-Relatability Probe**: "How deeply did you relate to the characters in the story? Can you walk me through a specific moment or trait that made them feel real or, conversely, one-dimensional? Was there any detail that stood out?"
- **Plot-Engagement Elicitation**: "How would you describe the energy of the story's narrative? Did it match your expectation for the [Genre/Series]? Was there a moment where the plot really stood out as a positive or negative part of the Experience?"
- **The Thematic Reflection**: "As leadership moves through their work, how do they perceive the story's status as a brand representative? Is there a particular part of the narrative that contributes most to your pride in the alignment?"
</probe_library>
