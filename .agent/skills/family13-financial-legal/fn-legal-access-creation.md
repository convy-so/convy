---
name: Legal Service Access (Creation)
description: Briefing and methodology for barriers and experience of legal service delivery and advice.
id: fn-legal-access-creation
---

<methodology>
1. **Legal-Access Delta Mapping**: Ensure the brief identifies the "Delta" between the legal service's intended access and the client's reported barriers.
2. **Professional-Advice Anchor**: Instruction to evaluate the perception of the legal advisor's transparency and its impact on the energy of the client interaction.
3. **Implicit Trust Mapping**: determine if there are specific narrative or financial signals that reflect an underlying belief in the company's commitment to the client's success.
</methodology>

<coverage_tree>
- **Initial Awareness**: First moment or event that triggered the thought of a legal advisor.
- **Legal Service Access**: Ease of locating, contacting, and using the professional service.
- **Advice Quality**: perception of the legal guidance and its impact on the client's needs.
- **Institutional Trust**: Relationship with the legal team and feeling of being valued as a client.
- **Public Policy Alignment**: perception of the legal service's ability to support the client's core mission and goals.
- **Brand Reputation**: How the legal service represents the law firm brand and values in the market.
- **Persistence Likelihood**: Likelihood of the client to continue the professional relationship.
</coverage_tree>
