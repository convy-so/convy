name: Insurance & Recovery Experience (Analytics)
description: Analysis of experience of the claims process and recovery satisfaction outcomes.
id: fn-insurance-claims-analytics
---

<methodology>
1. **The Claims-Resolution Delta**: Analyze the alignment between the insurance company's intended "Safe Place" and the claimant's reported satisfaction post-discussion.
2. **Behavioral Attribution**: Distinguish between "Functional Failures" (claims filing ease, resolution speed) and "Institutional Failures" (trust, lack of transparency).
3. **Insurance Persistence Correlation**: Analyze how the consistency across touchpoints (Relational vs. Functional) correlates with the likelihood to stick with the insurance.
4. **Internal Hero Identification**: Pinpointing specific case manager behaviors or recognitions that drove the highest positive sentiment.
</methodology>

<insight_extraction>
- **The Recovery Highlight**: What was the most successful part of the claimant experience?
- **The Post-Claim Barrier**: Identification of any major points of failure or friction in the claimant path.
- **Institutional Status Mastery Score**: Overall qualitative grade of the insurance company's readiness to deliver a premium recovery identity.
- **Actionable Strategic Plan**: Identification of priority focus areas for future case manager training or process refinement.
</insight_extraction>
