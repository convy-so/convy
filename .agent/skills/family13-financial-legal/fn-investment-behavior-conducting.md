<probe_library>
- **The Firm-Impression Elicitation**: "Thinking back to the last week of interaction with the [Investment Firm], what's the first word or feeling that comes to your mind? What was the context of that moment?"
- **The Risk-Efficacy Probe**: "How well do you perceive the company's ability to manage your individual risk levels or asset allocation? Can you walk me through the physical or digital signals that inform your sense of security? Was there any event or detail that stood out?"
- **Institutional Trust Elicitation**: "How would you describe your relationship with the advisor team? How did their influence or energy really stood out as a positive part of the client Experience? Was there a moment where their support really made a difference?"
- **The Transparency Reflection**: "As leadership moves through their work, how do they perceive the client's trust in the firm? Is there a particular part of the firm's communication that contributes most to your pride in the alignment?"
</probe_library>
