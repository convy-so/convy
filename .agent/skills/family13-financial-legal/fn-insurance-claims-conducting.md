<probe_library>
- **The Initial-Impression Elicitation**: "Thinking back to the first moment or event that made you file the claim with [Insurance Company], what was the spark? What was the context of that moment?"
- **The Claims-Filing Probe**: "How easy was it for you to navigate the initial claim reporting and documentation? Can you walk me through the physical or digital journey you took? Was there any barrier that stood out?"
- **Case-Manager Elicitation**: "How would you describe your relationship with the case manager team? How did their influence or energy really stood out as a positive part of the recovery Experience? Was there a moment where their support really made a difference?"
- **The Payout Reflection**: "As leadership moves through their work, how do they perceive the claimant's trust in the recovery process? Is there a particular part of the company's communication that contributes most to your pride in the alignment?"
</probe_library>
