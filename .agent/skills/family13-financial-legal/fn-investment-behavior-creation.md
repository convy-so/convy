name: Investment Behavior & Risk (Creation)
description: Briefing and methodology for drivers of risk-taking, asset allocation, and wealth management.
id: fn-investment-behavior-creation
---

<methodology>
1. **Risk-Reward Delta**: Ensure the brief identifies the "Delta" between the investor's intended risk and their reported security feeling post-investment.
2. **Wealth-Management Anchor**: Instruction to evaluate the perception of the wealth advisor's transparency and its impact on the energy of the client interaction.
3. **Implicit Success Mapping**: Determine if there are specific narrative or financial signals that reflect an underlying belief in the company's ability to deliver a premium identity.
</methodology>

<coverage_tree>
- **Initial Impression**: First word or feeling that comes to mind when the investment firm is mentioned.
- **Risk Efficacy**: perception of the company's ability to manage individual risk levels and asset allocation.
- **Support & Transparency**: How well the company's advice is perceived to match the investor's needs.
- **Relational Trust**: Relationship with the advisor team and feeling of being valued as a client.
- **Strategic Value**: Perception of the investment's ability to support the individual's core goals and wealth creation.
- **Brand Reputation**: How the firm represents the company brand and values in the market.
- **Persistence Likelihood**: Likelihood of the investor to continue the relationship in the long-term.
</coverage_tree>
