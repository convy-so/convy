---
name: Insurance & Recovery Experience (Creation)
description: Briefing and methodology for experience of the claims process and recovery satisfaction.
id: fn-insurance-claims-creation
---

<methodology>
1. **Claims-Resolution Delta**: Ensure the brief identifies the "Delta" between the insurance company's intended resolution and the claimant's reported satisfaction.
2. **Speed & Support Anchor**: Instruction to evaluate the perception of the claim handler's transparency and its impact on the energy of the persistence.
3. **Implicit Trust Mapping**: determine if there are specific narrative or financial signals that reflect an underlying belief in the company's commitment to the claimant's recovery.
</methodology>

<coverage_tree>
- **Initial Impression**: First word or feeling that comes to mind when the insurance company is mentioned.
- **Claims Filing Ease**: Ease of navigating the initial claim reporting and documentation.
- **Case Manager Support**: perception of the case manager's ability to provide the right support at the right time.
- **Resolution Speed**: perception of the company's ability to meet deadlines and project milestones.
- **Financial Fairness**: Perception of the payout or recovery matching the policy expectations.
- **Relational Trust**: Relationship with the insurance team and feeling of being valued as a claimant.
- **Post-Recovery Sentiment**: Overall qualitative grade of the company's readiness to deliver a premium recovery identity.
- **Recommendation Likelihood**: Likelihood of the claimant to recommend the insurance company's services to others.
</coverage_tree>
