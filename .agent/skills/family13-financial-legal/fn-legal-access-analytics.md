---
name: Legal Service Access (Analytics)
description: Analysis of barriers and experience of legal service delivery and advice outcomes.
id: fn-legal-access-analytics
---

<methodology>
1. **The Legal-Access Delta**: Analyze the alignment between the legal service's intended "Safe Place" and the client's reported security feeling post-discussion.
2. **Behavioral Attribution**: Distinguish between "Functional Failures" (advice quality, professional advocacy) and "Institutional Failures" (trust, lack of transparency).
3. **Legal Persistence Correlation**: Analyze how the consistency across touchpoints (Relational vs. Functional) correlates with the likelihood to stick with the legal team.
4. **Internal Hero Identification**: Pinpointing specific advisor behaviors or recognitions that drove the highest positive sentiment.
</methodology>

<insight_extraction>
- **The Recovery Highlight**: What was the most successful part of the client experience?
- **The Advisory Barrier**: Identification of any major points of failure or friction in the client path.
- **Institutional Status Mastery Score**: Overall qualitative grade of the law firm's readiness to deliver a premium identity.
- **Actionable Strategic Plan**: Identification of priority focus areas for future advisor training or process refinement.
</insight_extraction>
