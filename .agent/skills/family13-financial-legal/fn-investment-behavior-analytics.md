---

<methodology>
1. **The Risk-Reward Delta**: Analyze the alignment between the firm's intended "Safe Place" and the investor's reported security feeling post-discussion.
2. **Behavioral Attribution**: Distinguish between "Functional Failures" (risk manage, asset allocation) and "Relational Failures" (trust, lack of transparency).
3. **Investment Persistence Correlation**: Analyze how the consistency across touchpoints (Relational vs. Functional) correlates with the likelihood to stick with the firm.
4. **Internal Hero Identification**: Pinpointing specific advisor behaviors or recognitions that drove the highest positive sentiment.
</methodology>

<insight_extraction>
- **The Investment Highlight**: What was the most successful part of the client experience?
- **The Financial Barrier**: Identification of any major points of failure or friction in the investor path.
- **Institutional Status Mastery Score**: Overall qualitative grade of the investment firm's readiness to deliver a premium identity.
- **Actionable Strategic Plan**: Identification of priority focus areas for future advisor training or process refinement.
</insight_extraction>
