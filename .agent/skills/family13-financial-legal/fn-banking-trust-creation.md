name: Banking & Financial Trust (Creation)
description: Briefing and methodology for reliability, security, and institutional trust in finance.
id: fn-banking-trust-creation
---

<methodology>
1. **Financial-Security Delta**: Ensure the brief identifies the "Delta" between the bank's intended security and the customer's reported feeling of safety.
2. **Institutional Integrity Anchor**: Instruction to evaluate the perception of the financial institution's transparency and its impact on the energy of the client interaction.
3. **Internal Hero Identification**: determine if there are specific advisor behaviors or communication strategies that either encouraged or discouraged the customer's trust in the bank.
</methodology>

<coverage_tree>
- **Initial Impression**: First word or feeling that comes to mind when the bank is mentioned.
- **Institutional Trust**: How well the bank is perceived to have the client's best interests at heart.
- **Security Perception**: Feeling of safety regarding the client's assets and personal data.
- **Professional Advice Quality**: perception of the financial guidance and its impact on the energy of the client relationship.
- **Transparency & Communication**: Ease of locating, contacting, and trusting the financial institution.
- **Sense of Belonging**: Overall feeling of being valued and heard within the financial ecosystem.
- **Persistence Likelihood**: Likelihood of the client to continue the banking relationship or recommending it to others.
</coverage_tree>
