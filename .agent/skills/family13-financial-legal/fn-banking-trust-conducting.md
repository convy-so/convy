<probe_library>
- **The Bank-Impression Elicitation**: "Thinking back to the last week of interaction with the [Bank], what's the first word or feeling that comes to your mind? What was the context of that moment?"
- **The Security-Perception Probe**: "How secure do you feel regarding your assets and personal data with the bank? Can you walk me through the physical or digital signals that inform your sense of security? Was there any event or detail that stood out?"
- **Institutional Trust Elicitation**: "How would you describe your relationship with the financial advisor team? How did their influence or energy really stood out as a positive part of the client Experience? Was there a moment where their support really made a difference?"
- **The Transparency Reflection**: "As leadership moves through their work, how do they perceive the client's trust in the bank? Is there a particular part of the bank's communication that contributes most to your pride in the alignment?"
</probe_library>
