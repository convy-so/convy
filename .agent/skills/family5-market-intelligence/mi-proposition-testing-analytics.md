---
name: Proposition Testing (Analytics)
description: Analysis of new product or service concepts and value proposition outcomes.
id: mi-proposition-testing-analytics
---

<methodology>
1. **The Resonance Delta**: Analyze the alignment between the concept's original promise and the respondent's reported resonance post-discussion.
2. **Behavioral Attribution**: Distinguish between "Functional Liking" (it sounds nice) and "Behavioral Commitment" (I would replace my current solution).
3. **Price-Value Expectation Correlation**: Analyze how the respondent's willingness to pay correlates with their recognized problem-solution fit.
4. **Credibility Risk Assessment**: Identification of patterns that indicate a lack of belief in the product's promise or brand fit.
</methodology>

<insight_extraction>
- **The Concept Highlight**: What is the product's primary theoretical strength?
- **The Credibility Barrier**: Identification of any major points of failure or doubt.
- **Problem-Solution Mastery**: Overall qualitative grade of the concept's readiness to solve a market gap.
- **Actionable Proposition Plan**: Identification of priority focus areas for concept refinement or brand alignment.
</insight_extraction>
