name: Unmet Needs & Opportunity (Analytics)
description: Analysis of market gaps and opportunity outcomes.

---

<methodology>
1. **The Gap Identification**: Analyze the alignment between current solution failures and stated unmet needs post-usage period.
2. **Behavioral Attribution**: Distinguish between "Functional Needs" (tool must do X) and "Emotional Needs" (user must feel Y).
3. **Usage Frequency Correlation**: Analyze how unmet needs correlate with intended usage of a hypothetical new solution.
</methodology>

<insight_extraction>
- **The Core Unmet Need**: What is the most significant market gap?
- **The Ideal Solution Prototyping**: Compilation of features indicated for a "perfect" solution.
- **Mental Model Barriers**: Analysis of why existing solutions are failing and the habits that must be changed.
- **Actionable Opportunity Score**: Grade of the potential for a new solution in the specified problem domain.
</insight_extraction>
