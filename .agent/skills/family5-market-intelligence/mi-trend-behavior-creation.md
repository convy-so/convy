name: Trend & Emerging Behavior (Creation)
description: Briefing and methodology for identifying early signals of changing consumer behavior.
id: mi-trend-behavior-creation
---

<methodology>
1. **Lead-User Identification**: Ensure the brief targets "Early Adopters" or "Lead-Users" who are experimenting with new solutions at the edge of the category.
2. **Behavioral Divergence**: Instruction to identify where the respondent's behavior differs from the "Mainstream" ritual.
3. **Cultural Shift Mapping**: Determine the underlying cultural or social drivers (e.g., sustainability, digital nomadism) that are powering the emerging behavior.
</methodology>

<coverage_tree>
- **Initial Signal Detection**: The first moment or product that triggered the respondent's new behavior.
- **Mainstream Conflict**: In what specific way the respondent feels their current need is not met by traditional solutions.
- **Ritual Evolution**: How the respondent's daily habits have changed in response to the emerging trend.
- **Adoption Drivers**: The specific benefits (social, practical, emotional) that maintain the new behavior.
- **Future Intensity**: Likelihood of the behavior becoming more entrenched or spreading to others according to the respondent.
</coverage_tree>
