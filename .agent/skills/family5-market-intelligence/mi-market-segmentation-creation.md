---
name: Market Segmentation (Creation)
description: Briefing and methodology for identifying distinct user groups and their specific needs.
id: mi-market-segmentation-creation
---

<methodology>
1. **Human-Centered Grouping**: Ensure the brief focuses on meaningful differences in needs and behaviors rather than just demographics.
2. **Profile Depth**: Instruction to elicit the specific "Context of Use" and "Expected Result" for each potential segment.
3. **Internal vs. External Identification**: Identify if the segment is defined by a self-held identity (e.g., "The Perfectionist") or an external behavior (e.g., "The Casual User").
</methodology>

<coverage_tree>
- **Initial Profile Reaction**: How well the segment description matches the respondent's self-identity.
- **Specific Need Identification**: What does this segment require that other segments might not?
- **Usage Frequency & Intensity**: Pattern of product/service interaction for this specific group.
- **Switching Motivation**: What specific problem or benefit would cause this segment to change solutions?
- **Brand Resonance**: Overall qualitative grade of the brand's alignment with this segment's values.
</coverage_tree>
