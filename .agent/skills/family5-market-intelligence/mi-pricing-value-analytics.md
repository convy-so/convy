<methodology>
1. **The Price-Value Delta**: Analyze the alignment between the product's intended value proposition and the respondent's reported willingness to pay.
2. **Behavioral Attribution**: Distinguish between "Functional Value" (it saves time) and "Psychological Value" (it provides status or security).
3. **Value Anchor Correlation**: Analyze how the respondent's chosen comparison points (anchors) correlate with their price sensitivity.
4. **Risk Threshold Analysis**: Identification of price points that trigger quality concerns or excessive budget friction.
</methodology>

<insight_extraction>
- **The Value Highlight**: What is the most successful piece of the product's value proposition?
- **The Pricing Barrier**: Identification of any major points of failure or friction in the current pricing model.
- **Value-Resonance Score**: Overall qualitative grade of the product's readiness to deliver a premium result.
- **Actionable Pricing Plan**: Identification of priority focus areas for future price or value communication adjustment.
</insight_extraction>
