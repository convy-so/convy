## Interpretation Framework
**Goal:** Audit cognitive anchors. Is the brand distinct/defensible or commoditized/forgettable?
**Key Metric:** **Unaided vs. Aided Weighting.** Unaided recall (before name reveal) carries 3x weight.
**Principle:** "Truth" = what the consumer believes. Measure Gap: Intended (Brief) vs. Actual (Data).
**Quality Rule:** Convergence of adjectives (Independent use of same word by 40%+ of sample) defines the anchor.

## Critical Misreadings
- **The Aided Correction:** Over-weighting responses after the brand name was provided.
- **Category Blur:** High awareness but no emotional distinction from category average.
- **Communication Drift:** Recall of old campaigns over current ones.
- **Sentiment Bias:** Conflating general "positivity" with "brand trust."

## Equity/Awareness Matrix
| Status | Scenario | Interpretation |
|---|---|---|
| High Awareness / Neg Equity | Reputational Crisis | Known for the wrong reasons. PR/Comms priority. |
| Low Awareness / High Equity | Niche Leader | Top-of-funnel weakness. Marketing/Growth priority. |
| High Awareness / High Equity | Category Leader | Strategic defense. Maintain "shortcut for trust." |
| Low Awareness / Low Equity | Commodity | Total irrelevance. Position failure. |

## Synthesis & Reporting Patterns
| Pattern | Signal | Action |
|---|---|---|
| Adjective Convergence | 40%+ sample uses same word | Primary Positioning: This is the brand's mental real estate. |
| Divergent Cohorts | Gen Z vs. Boomer split | Segmented Positioning: Fix the cultural relevance gap. |
| Unaided Failure | Zero recall before reveal | Awareness Crisis: Ad spend / Category entry point failure. |
| Apathy Pulse | No strong positive/negative | Commoditization Risk: Lack of emotional anchor. |

## Synthesis Protocols
- **Step 1 (The Equity Map):** Plot awareness vs. sentiment types.
- **Step 2 (The Adjective Audit):** Rank top 5 unprompted associations. Compare to "Pillars."
- **Step 3 (The Recall Filter):** Separate campaign-driven associations from legacy-driven ones.
- **Step 4 (Substitution Cross-Check):** Link to the "Stock-Out" behavioral findings.

## Reporting Artifacts
**Part 1: Study Parameters** (Audience type, Customer status, Category Involvement).
**Part 2: Executive Summary** (Dominant positioning relative to category peers).
**Part 3: Awareness Pulse** (Unaided vs. Aided recall triggers).
**Part 4: Association Audit** (Adjective convergence, narrative maps, perceived weaknesses).
**Part 5: Decision Map Response** (Awareness vs. Equity intervention strategic link).

## Flagging Templates
**CONVERGENT ANCHOR:**
> "The brand's cognitive anchor is [Adjective]. This word appeared independently in 40%+ of the sample, indicating a strong, unified mental position."

**COMMODITY APATHY:**
> "Respondents demonstrated high apathy. No strong positive/negative associations found. Brand lacks a distinct emotional anchor for this audience; position is commoditized."

**UNAIDED RECALL FAILURE:**
> "Unaided recall was zero. The brand does not exist in the respondent's spontaneous category landscape. Awareness is strictly reactive (aided only)."
