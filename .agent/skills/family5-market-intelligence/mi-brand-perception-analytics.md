name: Brand Perception Research (Analytics)

---

<methodology>
1. **The Brand Image Delta**: Analyze the alignment between the brand's intended positioning and the respondent's reported implicit association and Personality.
2. **Behavioral Attribution**: Distinguish between "Functional Image" (good value) and "Emotional Resonance" (I feel like part of the group).
3. **Consideration-Set Convergence Correlation**: Analyze how the brand's placement in the hierarchy correlates with its competitive differentiator prominence.
4. **Brand Credibility Risk**: Identification of patterns that indicate a lack of belief in the brand's core mission or promise.
</methodology>

<insight_extraction>
- **The Brand Perception Highlight**: What is the most successful part of the brand image?
- **The Reputation Barrier**: Identification of any major points of failure or friction in the brand perception.
- **Brand Resonance Score**: Overall qualitative grade of the brand's readiness to deliver a premium identity.
- **Actionable Strategic Plan**: Identification of priority focus areas for future brand alignment or marketing refinement.
</insight_extraction>
