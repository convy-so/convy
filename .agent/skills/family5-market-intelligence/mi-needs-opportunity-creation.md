name: Unmet Needs & Opportunity (Creation)
description: Briefing and methodology for identifying market gaps and new product opportunities.
id: mi-needs-opportunity-creation
---

<methodology>
1. **The Gap Elicitation**: Ensure the brief clearly identifies the "Problem Domain" (e.g., Household cleaning, Financial planning).
2. **Current Solution Audit**: Instruction to identify what the respondent currently uses and where it fails.
3. **Ideal-State Visualisation**: Core focus on what an "effortless" or "perfect" solution would look like.
</methodology>

<coverage_tree>
- **Current Workflow**: How the respondent currently solves the problem.
- **Paint Points**: Specific frustrations with existing solutions.
- **Unmet Needs**: Desirable features or outcomes that current products don't provide.
- **Opportunity Assessment**: Likelihood to switch to or pay for a new solution.
- **Barriers to Entry**: Perception of the effort required to change habits or providers.
</coverage_tree>
