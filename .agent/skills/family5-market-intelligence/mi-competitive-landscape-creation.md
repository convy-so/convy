name: Competitive Landscape (Creation)
description: Briefing and methodology for competitive benchmarking and switching research.

---

<methodology>
1. **The Set Selection**: Ensure the brief clearly identifies the "Competitive Set" (e.g., Direct rivals, indirect alternatives).
2. **Switching Barrier Identification**: Instruction to explore what keeps the respondent with their current provider (Price, Habit, Feature lock-in).
3. **Winner-Takes-All Elicitation**: Identify which attributes would force a switch.
</methodology>

<coverage_tree>
- **Current Provider**: Who the respondent uses and for how long.
- **Top Alternative**: If they switch tomorrow, who would they choose?
- **Competitive Attributes**: Comparison on Price, Speed, Quality, Support, and Brand.
- **Switching Moment**: Analysis of what would trigger a provider change.
- **Brand Perception Delta**: Difference in emotional connection between current and alternative providers.
</coverage_tree>
