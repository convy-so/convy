<methodology>
1. **The Competitive Set Benchmarking**: Analyze the alignment between current provider performance and alternative candidate candidate perception.
2. **Dimension Delta Analysis**: Metric assessment of the Gap (Speed, Price, Quality) between current and primary alternative.
3. **Switching Propensity Correlation**: Analyze the correlation between friction scores and the stated likelihood of an account move.
4. **Brand Preference Over-index**: Identify attributes where a competitor is consistently chosen as the "Standard" or "Preferred" over the incumbent.
</methodology>

<insight_extraction>
- **The Competitive Winner**: Who is the primary alternative for most respondents?
- **The Switching Trigger**: What is the most consistent reason for considering a move?
- **Incumbent Weakness**: Analysis of the attributes where the current provider is most vulnerable.
- **Brand Preference Delta**: Analysis of the emotional connection versus functional performance between providers.
</insight_extraction>
