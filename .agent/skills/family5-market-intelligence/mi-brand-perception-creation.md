<methodology>
1. **Implicit Association Mapping**: Ensure the brief focuses on "gut-feeling" associations and emotional resonance rather than just functional awareness.
2. **Competitive Context**: Instruction to identify where the brand sits in the respondent's "Consideration Set" versus direct and indirect alternatives.
3. **Trust & Credibility Threshold**: Determine the specific brand signal (e.g., heritage, innovation, sustainability) that is intended to drive the highest trust.
</methodology>

<coverage_tree>
- **Initial Brand Image**: The first word or feeling that comes to mind when the brand is mentioned.
- **Brand Personality**: How the respondent describes the brand's character if it were a person.
- **Consideration-Set Placement**: Where the brand fits in the respondent's hierarchy of choice.
- **Differentiator Insight**: What single attribute or promise makes the brand stand out (or fail to).
- **Brand Resonance**: Overall qualitative grade of the brand's alignment with the respondent's values.
- **Credibility Score**: How believable the brand's core promise feels.
</coverage_tree>
