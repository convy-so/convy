---
name: Proposition Testing (Creation)
description: Briefing and methodology for testing new product or service concepts and value propositions.
id: mi-proposition-testing-creation
---

<methodology>
1. **The Concept Delta**: Ensure the brief identifies the "Proposition" (what are we promising?) versus the "Conceptual Context" (where would this fit in the market?).
2. **Behavioral Commitment Probe**: Instruction to find the "Willingness to Switch" rather than just a theoretical liking for the idea.
3. **Resonance Threshold**: Define the specific benefit or messaging that is intended to drive the highest resonance.
</methodology>

<coverage_tree>
- **Initial Concept Reaction**: First impression of the core promise and visual/textual delivery.
- **Benefit Prioritization**: Which specific promise or feature is the single most attractive?
- **Competitive Conflict**: How the new proposition compares to the respondent's current solution.
- **Credibility Signals**: Does the proposition feel like it could genuinely be delivered by the brand?
- **Concept Longevity**: Likelihood of the respondent to continue considering the proposition after the initial interaction.
</coverage_tree>
