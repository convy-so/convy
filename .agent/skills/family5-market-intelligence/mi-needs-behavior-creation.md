<methodology>
1. **The "Why" Behind the "What"**: Ensure the brief focuses on motivations rather than just actions. Ask about rituals and emotional drivers.
2. **Current Solution Audit**: Identify how the respondent currently solves the problem, including "compensating behaviors" or workarounds.
3. **Social Influence Mapping**: Determine the role of external social pressures or recommendations in the decision-making process.
</methodology>

<coverage_tree>
- **Core Motivations**: The underlying psychological or practical needs driving the category behavior.
- **Habits & Rituals**: The specific, often unconscious, patterns of usage or purchase.
- **Compensating Behaviors**: Current workarounds or frustrations with existing solutions.
- **Decision Drivers**: Factors like price, quality, convenience, or brand status.
- **Social & Environmental Context**: How the physical or social environment shapes behavior.
</coverage_tree>
