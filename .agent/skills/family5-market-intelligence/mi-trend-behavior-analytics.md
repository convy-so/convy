<methodology>
1. **The Behavioral Divergence Delta**: Analyze the alignment between the mainstream ritual and the respondent's reported new behavior.
2. **Behavioral Attribution**: Distinguish between "Functional Advantages" (it's faster) and "Cultural Drivers" (it makes me feel better).
3. **Trend Velocity Correlation**: Analyze how the respondent's perceived future adoption correlates with their current behavior complexity.
4. **Mainstream-Switching Risk**: Identification of patterns that indicate a potential for the trend to spread to the mainstream audience.
</methodology>

<insight_extraction>
- **The Emerging Signal Highlight**: What was the most successful part of the new behavior?
- **The Resistance Barrier**: Identification of any major points of failure or friction for the trend's wider adoption.
- **Trend Resilience Score**: Overall qualitative grade of the behavior's readiness to become mainstream.
- **Actionable Strategic Plan**: Identification of priority focus areas for future brand alignment or innovation refinement.
</insight_extraction>
