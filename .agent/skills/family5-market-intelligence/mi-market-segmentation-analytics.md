---
name: Market Segmentation (Analytics)
description: Analysis of distinct user groups and their specific needs.
id: mi-market-segmentation-analytics
---

<methodology>
1. **The Segment-Need Delta**: Analyze the alignment between the target segment's identified needs and the product's delivery.
2. **Behavioral Attribution**: Distinguish between "Demographic Markers" and "Motivational Drivers" for each segment.
3. **Usage-Intensity Correlation**: Analyze how the frequency of use correlates with the specific segment's needs and pain points.
4. **Segment Profitability Risk**: Identification of patterns that indicate high-cost/low-value segments versus high-growth opportunities.
</methodology>

<insight_extraction>
- **The Core Segment Highlight**: What is the most successful part of the brand's alignment with its primary audience?
- **The Segment-Barrier**: Identification of any major points of failure or friction for a specific sub-group.
- **Audience Mastery Score**: Overall qualitative grade of the brand's readiness to serve its target market.
- **Actionable Segmentation Plan**: Identification of priority focus areas for future marketing or product refinement for specific groups.
</insight_extraction>
