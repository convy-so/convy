<methodology>
1. **Decision Map Alignment**: Ensure the brief clearly distinguishes between "Brand NPS" (overall relationship) and "Transactional NPS" (specific event).
2. **Audience Stratification**: Instruction to identify Promoters (9-10), Passives (7-8), and Detractors (0-6) in the sampling plan.
3. **Primary Objective**: Elicit the "Why" behind the score to identify structural business drivers.
</methodology>

<coverage_tree>
- **The Score**: Mandatory 0-10 Likert scale.
- **Root Cause**: Qualitative explanation for the score.
- **Brand Drivers**: Price, Quality, Support, Communication, and Innovation.
- **Switching Risk**: Likelihood to consider competitors in the next 12 months.
</coverage_tree>
