<methodology>
1. **Driver Identification**: Correlate qualitative feedback with the 0-10 score to identify the primary drivers of advocacy and detraction.
2. **Structural vs. Situational**: Categorize negative signals as either a "Systemic Failure" (requiring product/process change) or a "One-off Friction" (requiring service recovery).
3. **Switching Risk Assessment**: Flag respondents whose feedback mentions competitors or price-sensitivity as high switching risks.
</methodology>

<insight_extraction>
- **The Core Promoters**: What specifically makes them advocates? (Isolate the 'Peak' moment).
- **The Core Detractors**: What is the single biggest barrier to their satisfaction?
- **Tactical vs. Strategic**: Distinguish between immediate fixes (bugs/speed) and long-term brand challenges (value/trust).
- **Actionable Routing**: Map insights to Product, Marketing, or Customer Success teams.
</insight_extraction>
