## Interpretation Framework
**Goal:** Determine if interaction produced the "Service Recovery Paradox" (elevated trust) or merely a functional fix.
**Principle:** Functional-Emotional Split. A "Satisfied" functional outcome (fixed) is the floor; Emotion (trust restored) is the goal.
**The Effort Deduction:** High resolution effort ("called 5 times") is a process failure despite functional success.
**Quality Rule:** Staff Ownership ("I will handle this" vs. "I'll put a ticket in") is the #1 driver of trust.

## Critical Misreadings
- **The Sympathy Gap:** High empathy (staff was warm) + Low functional resolution (problem exists).
- **Fairness Performance:** Respondent saying "they tried their best" to be polite, masking a poor outcome.
- **Gratitude Confound:** Trust inflated by a gift (voucher) rather than the recovery process.
- **Fragmented Ownership:** Multi-contact failure where no single agent took responsibility.

## Evidence Hierarchy
| Tier | Title | Evidence Type |
|---|---|---|
| Tier 1 | Behavioral (1.00) | Specific ownership/empathy behaviors named + Post-recovery trust delta established. |
| Tier 2 | Narrated (0.70) | Chronological account + Functional result established + General sentiment. |
| Tier 3 | Pulse (0.4) | General opinion of recovery without specific stage anchoring. |
| Tier 4 | Zero | No failure/recovery narrative. Session disconnect. |

## Synthesis & Reporting Patterns
| Pattern | Signal | Action |
|---|---|---|
| Recovery Paradox | Higher trust post-fix than before failure | Training Asset: Isolate the "Magic Moment" behaviors. |
| The Cold Fix | Problem resolved / Emotionally cold | Empathy Training: Focus on impact acknowledgment. |
| Effort Depletion | Resolved but required high customer chase | Process Fix: Implement proactive status triggers. |
| The Failed Recovery | Pre-fail Trust High / Post-fix Trust Low | Critical Risk: Recovery made things worse. Immediate "Save" play. |

## Scoring Weighting
- **Ownership Multiplier (x1.5 on Emotional):** Weight emotional restoration higher if SE-03 Ownership is confirmed.
- **Effort Penalty (-0.20):** Subtract from overall sentiment if SE-02 Help-seeking required >2 contacts.

## Reporting Artifacts
**Part 1: Executive Summary** (Trust Verdict & Primary Driver).
**Part 2: Failure Context** (Severity and starting trust baseline).
**Part 3: The Interaction** (Functional Fix vs. Emotional Restoration).
**Part 4: Effort & Help-Seeking** (Friction in entry channel / Total exertion).
**Part 5: Decision Map Response** (Training vs. Process fixes).

## Escalation Protocols
- **Failed Recovery Alarm:** Pre-failure trust High / Post-recovery trust Low.
- **Ownership Void:** Multiple reports of "Not my job" or procedural scripts.
- **Unresolved Critical Failure:** Severity High + Functional Resolution False (Legal/Safety).

## Reporting Flag Templates
**GRATITUDE CONFOUND:**
> "Findings may be inflated by a gratitude confound; the respondent received a compensatory gesture prior to research. Emotional restoration findings are weighted toward process friction observations."

**UNCONFIRMED COMPLETION:**
> "The recovery episode is not yet complete. Trust findings are provisional and represent the respondent's current sentiment during an ongoing dispute."

**FUNC-EMOTIONAL DIVERGENCE:**
> "Respondent reports a successful functional fix but zero emotional restoration. The brand solved the problem but failed the relationship. High hidden attrition risk."
