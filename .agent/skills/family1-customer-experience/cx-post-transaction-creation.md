---
name: Post-Transaction Research (Creation)
description: Study specific, recent transactions and the experience surrounding them.
id: cx-post-transaction-creation
---

<methodology>
1. **Journey-Based Grounding**: Anchor every question to a specific interaction (e.g., "thinking about your purchase on Tuesday").
2. **Expectation vs. Reality**: Probe the gap between what the customer expected and what they received.
3. **Touchpoint Diagnostics**: Identify which specific part of the journey (Discovery, Checkout, Delivery, Use) failed or succeeded.
</methodology>

<coverage_tree>
- **The Event**: Proof of transaction and date.
- **The Process**: Satisfaction at each stage (Discovery to Fulfillment).
- **Checkout Friction**: Ease of purchase and transparency of terms.
- **Delivery/Fulfillment**: Speed, communication, and state of product/service.
- **Immediate Result**: Meeting or failing the customer's need.
</coverage_tree>
