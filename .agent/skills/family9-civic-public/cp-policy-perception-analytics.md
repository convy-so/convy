---
name: Policy Perception & Impact (Analytics)
description: Analysis of understanding and sentiment toward new policies or regulations outcomes.
id: cp-policy-perception-analytics
---

<methodology>
1. **The Policy-Understanding Delta**: Analyze the alignment between the intended policy message and the citizen's reported understanding post-discussion.
2. **Behavioral Attribution**: Distinguish between "Functional Failures" (impact perception, compliance) and "Institutional Failures" (trust, lack of transparency).
3. **Policy Persistence Correlation**: Analyze how the consistency across touchpoints (Relational vs. Functional) correlates with the likelihood to comply with the new regulation.
4. **Internal Hero Identification**: Pinpointing specific community behaviors or recognitions that drove the highest positive sentiment.
</methodology>

<insight_extraction>
- **The Policy Highlight**: What was the most successful part of the policy experience?
- **The Regulatory Barrier**: Identification of any major points of failure or friction in the policy path.
- **Institutional Status Mastery Score**: Overall qualitative grade of the government/institution's readiness to deliver a premium community identity.
- **Actionable Strategic Plan**: Identification of priority focus areas for future policy training or process refinement.
</insight_extraction>
