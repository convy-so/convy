---
name: Citizen Service Experience (Analytics)
description: Analysis of public service accessibility and satisfaction level outcomes.
id: cp-citizen-service-analytics
---

<methodology>
1. **The Public-Realm Delta**: Analyze the alignment between the service's intended "Public Value" and the citizen's reported emotion post-discussion.
2. **Behavioral Attribution**: Distinguish between "Functional Failures" (accessibility, efficacy) and "Institutional Failures" (trust, lack of transparency).
3. **Service Persistence Correlation**: Analyze how the consistency across touchpoints (Relational vs. Functional) correlates with the likelihood to re-engage with the public services.
4. **Internal Hero Identification**: Pinpointing specific public servants or behaviors that drove the highest positive sentiment or safety feeling.
</methodology>

<insight_extraction>
- **The Service Highlight**: What was the most successful part of the delivery experience?
- **The Public Barrier**: Identification of any major points of failure or friction in the public realm path.
- **Institutional Status Mastery Score**: Overall qualitative grade of the government/institution's readiness to deliver a premium identity.
- **Actionable Strategic Plan**: Identification of priority focus areas for future public service training or process refinement.
</insight_extraction>
