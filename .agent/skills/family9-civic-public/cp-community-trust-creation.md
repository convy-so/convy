---
name: Community Trust & Safety (Creation)
description: Briefing and methodology for trust in local government and institutional safety perception.
id: cp-community-trust-creation
---

<methodology>
1. **Safety & Belonging Delta**: Ensure the brief identifies the "Delta" between the community's intended safety and the citizen's reported security feeling.
2. **Transparency & Governance Anchor**: Instruction to evaluate the perception of the local government's transparency and its impact on the energy of the community interaction.
3. **Public Hero Identification**: Determine if there are specific public servants or behaviors that either encouraged or discouraged the citizen's trust in the institution.
</methodology>

<coverage_tree>
- **Initial Impression**: First word or feeling that comes to mind when the local government is mentioned.
- **Institutional Trust**: How well the local government is perceived to have the community's best interests at heart.
- **Safety Perception**: Feeling of security during the day and night in the public realm.
- **Police & Governance Impact**: perception of the institutional support and its impact on the energy of the community.
- **Transparency & Communication**: Ease of locating, contacting, and trusting the local government.
- **Sense of Belonging**: Overall feeling of being valued and heard within the community.
- **Public Policy Alignment**: perception of the local government's ability to support the community's core mission and goals.
</coverage_tree>
