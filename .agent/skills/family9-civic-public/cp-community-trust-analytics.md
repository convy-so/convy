name: Community Trust & Safety (Analytics)

---

<methodology>
1. **The Safety & Belonging Delta**: Analyze the alignment between the community's intended "Safe Place" and the citizen's reported security feeling post-discussion.
2. **Behavioral Attribution**: Distinguish between "Functional Failures" (policing, governance impact) and "Institutional Failures" (trust, transparency).
3. **Community Persistence Correlation**: Analyze how the consistency across touchpoints (Relational vs. Functional) correlates with the likelihood to stick with the community.
4. **Internal Hero Identification**: Pinpointing specific community behaviors or recognitions that drove the highest positive sentiment.
</methodology>

<insight_extraction>
- **The Community Highlight**: What was the most successful part of the community experience?
- **The Public Barrier**: Identification of any major points of failure or friction in the community path.
- **Institutional Reputation Mastery Score**: Overall qualitative grade of the government/institution's readiness to deliver a premium community identity.
- **Actionable Strategic Plan**: Identification of priority focus areas for future community building or transparency refinement.
</insight_extraction>
