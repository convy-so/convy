name: Voter Sentiment & Participation (Creation)
description: Briefing and methodology for drivers of democratic participation and civic engagement.
id: cp-voter-sentiment-creation
---

<methodology>
1. **Participation Delta Mapping**: Ensure the brief identifies the "Delta" between the citizen's intended democratic participation and their reported barriers.
2. **Civic Engagement Anchor**: Instruction to evaluate the perception of the organizational culture and its impact on the energy of the community interaction.
3. **Public Hero Identification**: Determine if there are specific public servant behaviors or communication strategies that either encouraged or discouraged the citizen's trust in the election.
</methodology>

<coverage_tree>
- **Initial Impression**: First word or feeling that comes to mind when the election is mentioned.
- **Participation Awareness**: Clarity and perceived relevance of the election's objectives.
- **Civic Engagement Efficacy**: How well the democratic process is perceived to meet the community's needs.
- **Institutional Trust**: Relationship with the election team and feeling of being valued as a citizen.
- **Public Policy Alignment**: perception of the election's ability to support the community's core mission and goals.
- **Voter Motivation**: Drivers of participation and civic engagement levels.
- **Future Commitment**: Likelihood of the citizen to continue participating in the democratic process.
</coverage_tree>
