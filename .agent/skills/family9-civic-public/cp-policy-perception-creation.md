---
name: Policy Perception & Impact (Creation)
description: Briefing and methodology for understanding and sentiment toward new policies or regulations.
id: cp-policy-perception-creation
---

<methodology>
1. **Policy Understanding Delta**: Ensure the brief identifies the "Delta" between the intended policy message and the citizen's reported understanding.
2. **Impact & Compliance Anchor**: Instruction to evaluate the perception of the policy's impact and its impact on the energy of the community interaction.
3. **Public Hero Identification**: Determine if there are specific public servant behaviors or communication strategies that either encouraged or discouraged the citizen's trust in the policy.
</methodology>

<coverage_tree>
- **Initial Awareness**: First moment or event that triggered the awareness of the new policy.
- **Policy Understanding**: Clarity and perceived relevance of the policy's objectives.
- **Impact Perception**: Feeling of the policy's effect on the respondent's daily life or business.
- **Institutional Trust**: Relationship with the policy team and feeling of being valued as a citizen.
- **Public Policy Alignment**: perception of the policy's ability to support the community's core mission and goals.
- **Compliance Willingness**: Likelihood of the citizen to comply with the new regulation or policy.
- **Sense of Belonging**: Overall feeling of being valued and heard within the policy framework.
</coverage_tree>
