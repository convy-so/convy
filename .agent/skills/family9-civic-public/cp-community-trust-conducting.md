<probe_library>
- **The Community Impression Elicitation**: "Thinking back to the last interaction you had with the [Local Government], what's the first word or feeling that comes to your mind? What was the context of that moment?"
- **The Safety Probe**: "How secure do you feel when walking through your neighborhood after dark? Can you walk me through the physical or social signals that inform your sense of security? Was there an event or detail that stood out?"
- **Institutional Trust Elicitation**: "How would you describe your relationship with the local government team? How did their influence or energy really stood out as a positive part of the community Energy? Was there a moment where their support really made a difference?"
- **The Transparency Reflection**: "As leadership moves through their work, how do they perceive the community's trust? Is there a particular part of the government's communication that contributes most to your pride in the alignment?"
</probe_library>
