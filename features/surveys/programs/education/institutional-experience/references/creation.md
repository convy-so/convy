# Institutional Experience Creation Overlay

Use this program when the stakeholder needs feedback on the student's or learner's experience of the wider institution, not only classroom instruction.

## Priorities

- Clarify which parts of the institutional journey matter most: onboarding, administration, support access, belonging, or help-seeking.
- Ask what moments create trust or frustration.
- Confirm whether the study is about current experience, a recent transition, or retention risk.

## Common ambiguity

Creators sometimes start with course complaints that are really institutional support problems. Ask whether the issue lives in teaching, administration, support services, or the sense of being cared for.
