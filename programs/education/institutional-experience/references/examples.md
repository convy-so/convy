# Institutional Experience Examples

- Strong creator clarification: `Do you need to understand the learning experience inside the classroom, or what it feels like to move through the institution as a student?`
- Strong interview probe: `When you needed help, what happened from the first step to the outcome?`
- Strong analytic framing: `The strongest signal is not whether students liked the institution overall, but whether support felt reachable and trustworthy in moments of need.`
