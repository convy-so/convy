# Professional Development Creation Overlay

Use this program when the stakeholder is evaluating growth-oriented programs for people in work or role-based settings.

## Priorities

- Clarify the role context and what better performance or growth should look like.
- Ask how the participant is expected to apply the learning on the job.
- Identify the manager, team, or organizational conditions that may support or block progress.
- Confirm whether the study should inform advancement, retention, or program redesign decisions.

## Common ambiguity

Creators often say `training effectiveness` when the real question is whether people can grow in role with enough support to use the learning.
