# Course Efficacy Creation Overlay

Use this program when the stakeholder wants to know whether a course or learning experience built usable mastery.

## Priorities

- Clarify what learners were supposed to be able to do after the experience.
- Separate course enjoyment from demonstrated capability.
- Ask what real tasks, assignments, or situations matter most.
- Pin down whether the study should focus on instruction quality, practice quality, or transfer confidence.

## Common ambiguity

Creators often say `effectiveness` when they only mean reactions. If that happens, ask whether they need evidence of mastery, confidence to apply, or general course sentiment.
