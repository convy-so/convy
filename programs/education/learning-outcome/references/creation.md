# Learning Outcome Creation Overlay

Use this program when the stakeholder cares about what changed after learning over time.

## Priorities

- Clarify the lag between the learning experience and the feedback collection point.
- Ask what durable behavior change or retention they need to observe.
- Identify the environment in which learners tried to apply the learning.
- Confirm whether managers, peers, or systems may shape success.

## Common ambiguity

Creators often blend immediate course evaluation with post-course impact. Ask whether they need feedback on the course itself or on what happened after participants returned to real work or study.
